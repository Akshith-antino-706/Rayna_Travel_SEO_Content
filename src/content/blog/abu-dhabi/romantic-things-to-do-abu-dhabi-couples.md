# Romantic Activities for Couples in Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the United Arab Emirates, offers an array of romantic experiences for couples seeking both luxury and charm. Whether you’re looking for serene sunset spots, intimate dining, or cultural explorations, Abu Dhabi promises unforgettable moments. Here’s a comprehensive guide to the most romantic activities for couples in this vibrant city.

## Sunset Spots

### 1. Corniche Beach

**Why it's Romantic:** The Corniche Beach provides a stunning backdrop for romantic walks with its pristine sands and panoramic views of the Arabian Gulf. Watching the sun set over the water is a captivating experience.

- **Cost for Two:** Free
- **How to Book:** No booking required
- **Best Time:** Arrive around 5:00 PM to secure a good spot
- **Tip:** Bring a blanket and some snacks for a cozy beach picnic as you enjoy the sunset.

### 2. Observation Deck at 300

**Why it's Romantic:** Positioned on the 74th floor of Etihad Towers, the Observation Deck at 300 offers breathtaking views of Abu Dhabi’s skyline and the Arabian Gulf, making it a perfect setting for a romantic evening.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 190 (approx. USD 52)
- **How to Book:** Tickets can be purchased on-site or through the Etihad Towers website
- **Best Time:** Around 6:00 PM to catch the sunset
- **Insider Tip:** Enjoy high tea while soaking in the views for an added touch of elegance.

## Romantic Restaurants

### 3. Bord Eau

**Why it's Romantic:** Located in the Shangri-La Hotel, Bord Eau offers exquisite French cuisine in an intimate setting, ideal for a candle-lit dinner.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 800-1000 (approx. USD 218-272)
- **How to Book:** Reservations can be made via the Shangri-La website or by phone
- **Best Time:** Dinner reservations are recommended around 7:30 PM
- **Insider Tip:** Request a table by the window for a view of the Grand Mosque illuminated at night.

### 4. BBQ Al Qasr

**Why it's Romantic:** Set on a private beach, BBQ Al Qasr offers a unique dining experience with private cabanas, allowing couples to enjoy grilled delicacies under the stars.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 600-800 (approx. USD 163-218)
- **How to Book:** Reservations are essential and can be made through the Emirates Palace website
- **Best Time:** Evenings are best for dining under the stars
- **Tip:** Opt for the seafood platter for a taste of local fresh catches.

## Couples Spa

### 5. Anantara Spa

**Why it's Romantic:** Located on the Saadiyat Island, Anantara Spa offers luxurious couples’ treatments in serene surroundings, perfect for relaxation and rejuvenation.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 1200 (approx. USD 327) for a 90-minute session
- **How to Book:** Reservations can be made through the Anantara website or by phone
- **Best Time:** Late afternoon sessions allow you to unwind before dinner
- **Insider Tip:** Book a private treatment room for added privacy and tranquility.

### 6. Talise Spa

**Why it's Romantic:** Situated in Jumeirah at Etihad Towers, Talise Spa provides a haven of serenity with customized couples’ spa packages.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 1000 (approx. USD 272) for a 60-minute session
- **How to Book:** Online booking through the Jumeirah website is recommended
- **Best Time:** Morning sessions are quieter and more peaceful
- **Tip:** Combine your spa day with a visit to the Observation Deck at 300 for a complete experience.

## Private Tours

### 7. Private Dhow Cruise

**Why it's Romantic:** Experience the traditional side of Abu Dhabi with a private dhow cruise along the Corniche, offering stunning views of the cityscape from the water.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 700 (approx. USD 190)
- **How to Book:** Book through local tour operators such as Captain Tony’s
- **Best Time:** Sunset cruises offer the most romantic ambiance
- **Insider Tip:** Opt for a cruise that includes dinner for an all-inclusive romantic evening.

### 8. Desert Safari for Two

**Why it's Romantic:** Explore the enchanting desert landscape on a private safari, complete with dune bashing, camel rides, and a secluded dinner under the stars.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 1500 (approx. USD 408)
- **How to Book:** Book through reputable tour operators like Arabian Adventures
- **Best Time:** Afternoon tours to catch the sunset over the dunes
- **Tip:** Wear comfortable clothing and bring a camera for stunning desert photos.

## Scenic Walks

### 9. Al Ain Oasis

**Why it's Romantic:** Al Ain Oasis offers lush greenery and shaded walkways, providing a peaceful escape from the city hustle. It’s a UNESCO World Heritage site, making it both romantic and culturally significant.

- **Cost for Two:** Free
- **How to Book:** No booking required
- **Best Time:** Early morning or late afternoon to avoid the heat
- **Insider Tip:** Bring a guidebook or download an app to learn about the oasis’s history and flora.

### 10. Saadiyat Beach Walk

**Why it's Romantic:** Stroll hand-in-hand along the Saadiyat Beach, where the pristine sands and turquoise waters create a picturesque setting.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 50 (approx. USD 14) for beach access
- **How to Book:** Entrance tickets available at the beach kiosk
- **Best Time:** Late afternoon for cooler temperatures and beautiful lighting
- **Tip:** Pack a picnic to enjoy on the beach after your walk.

## Rooftop Dining

### 11. Ray's Bar

**Why it's Romantic:** Located on the 62nd floor of Jumeirah at Etihad Towers, Ray's Bar offers spectacular city views, perfect for a romantic evening with cocktails and light bites.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 300-500 (approx. USD 82-136)
- **How to Book:** Reservations recommended via the Jumeirah website
- **Best Time:** Go around 8:00 PM for a vibrant atmosphere
- **Insider Tip:** Try their signature cocktails for a unique taste experience.

## Boat Rides

### 12. Yas Marina Circuit Yacht Tour

**Why it's Romantic:** Sail around Yas Island and experience the thrill of passing by the Yas Marina Circuit, especially beautiful during the evening with the circuit lights on.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 1000 (approx. USD 272)
- **How to Book:** Book through Yas Marina’s official website or local tour companies
- **Best Time:** Evening tours offer the best views
- **Tip:** Bring a light jacket as it can get breezy on the water.

## Cultural Experiences for Two

### 13. Louvre Abu Dhabi

**Why it's Romantic:** Explore the stunning galleries and architecture of Louvre Abu Dhabi hand in hand. The museum’s dome allows for beautiful light displays, creating a magical atmosphere.

- **Cost for Two:** AED 120 (approx. USD 33)
- **How to Book:** Tickets available on the Louvre Abu Dhabi website
- **Best Time:** Late afternoon to evening when the museum is less crowded
- **Insider Tip:** Don’t miss the rooftop café for a romantic pause with views of the water.

## Photography Spots

### 14. Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

**Why it's Romantic:** One of the world’s largest mosques, its majestic white domes and intricate architecture make for stunning photos, especially at sunset.

- **Cost for Two:** Free
- **How to Book:** No booking required, but guided tours can be booked
- **Best Time:** Sunset for the best lighting
- **Tip:** Respect the dress code and bring a camera with a good zoom lens for detailed shots.

## Romantic Hotels

### 15. Qasr Al Sarab Desert Resort

**Why it's Romantic:** Nestled in the Liwa Desert, this luxury resort offers a secluded retreat with elegant rooms overlooking the sand dunes.

- **Cost for Two:** Starting from AED 2000 (approx. USD 544) per night
- **How to Book:** Book through the Anantara website or trusted travel sites
- **Best Time:** Cooler months from November to March
- **Insider Tip:** Book a room with a private terrace to enjoy the desert views in privacy.

## Conclusion

Abu Dhabi is a city that beautifully blends tradition with modern luxury, offering couples an array of romantic experiences to suit all tastes and budgets. From serene beach walks and luxurious spa days to cultural explorations and stunning dining experiences, the city promises unforgettable moments for every couple. Whether you choose to indulge in a high-end adventure or explore the city's natural beauty, Abu Dhabi is sure to capture your hearts.
