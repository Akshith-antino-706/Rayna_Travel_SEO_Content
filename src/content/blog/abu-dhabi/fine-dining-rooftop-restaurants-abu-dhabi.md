# Fine Dining and Rooftop Restaurant Guide for Abu Dhabi

Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates, is a city that exudes luxury and sophistication, offering a plethora of fine dining experiences and breathtaking rooftop views. Whether you're celebrating a special occasion, hosting a business dinner, or simply indulging in culinary excellence, this guide will help you navigate the best fine dining and rooftop restaurants in Abu Dhabi. From Michelin-starred venues to unique dining concepts, here are the top places to consider.

## Michelin-Starred Magnificence

### 1. 99 Sushi Bar & Restaurant

- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary Japanese
- **Price Range:** AED 400-600 per person (Approx. $109-$163)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Highly recommended, especially for weekend evenings
- **Best Dishes:** Wagyu Tartar, Toro Nigiri, Butterfish with Truffle
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Overlooks the Abu Dhabi waterfront, offering a sleek and modern interior
- **Location:** The Galleria, Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi

99 Sushi Bar & Restaurant is a Michelin-starred venue known for its exquisite Japanese dishes. The restaurant's minimalist design and waterfront views make it a perfect choice for a romantic dinner or a refined business meeting.

**Tip:** For an unforgettable experience, consider the Omakase menu, which allows the chef to surprise you with a selection of signature dishes.

### 2. Hakkasan Abu Dhabi

- **Cuisine Type:** Cantonese
- **Price Range:** AED 500-700 per person (Approx. $136-$190)
- **Dress Code:** Elegant
- **Reservation Tips:** Book in advance, especially during weekends
- **Best Dishes:** Peking Duck with Caviar, Crispy Duck Salad, Dim Sum Platter
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Located in the Emirates Palace, offering a luxurious setting with traditional Chinese decor
- **Location:** Emirates Palace, West Corniche Road, Abu Dhabi

Hakkasan is renowned for its modern take on Cantonese cuisine, set within the opulent surroundings of the Emirates Palace. This is an ideal venue for celebrations or impressing business clients.

**Insider Tip:** Try their weekend brunch for a delightful array of signature dishes accompanied by an extensive beverage selection.

## Rooftop Dining with a View

### 3. Ray's Grill

- **Cuisine Type:** Steakhouse
- **Price Range:** AED 300-500 per person (Approx. $82-$136)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Recommended for dinner; request a window seat for the best views
- **Best Dishes:** Wagyu Beef, Black Angus Tenderloin, Truffle Mashed Potatoes
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Located on the 63rd floor of Jumeirah at Etihad Towers, offering panoramic city views
- **Location:** Jumeirah at Etihad Towers, Corniche Road, Abu Dhabi

Ray's Grill is a top choice for steak lovers and those looking to enjoy stunning views of Abu Dhabi's skyline. Its high altitude and elegant setting make it perfect for a memorable dining experience.

**Tip:** Visit right before sunset to watch the city transition from day to night.

### 4. AT25

- **Cuisine Type:** International
- **Price Range:** AED 250-400 per person (Approx. $68-$109)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Book a table on the terrace for the best experience
- **Best Dishes:** Grilled Seafood Platter, Angus Beef Burger, Mezze Platter
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Situated on the 25th floor, offers a chic ambiance with stunning views of the city
- **Location:** Marriott Hotel Downtown Abu Dhabi, Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Street

AT25 is the perfect spot for those who enjoy chic rooftop settings with a vibrant atmosphere. The restaurant offers an eclectic menu and serves as a fantastic backdrop for celebrations and casual gatherings.

**Insider Tip:** Their signature cocktails are a must-try, perfectly complementing the stunning views.

## Celebrity Chef Creations

### 5. Zuma Abu Dhabi

- **Cuisine Type:** Japanese Izakaya
- **Price Range:** AED 400-600 per person (Approx. $109-$163)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Elegant
- **Reservation Tips:** Advance booking is advised, particularly on weekends
- **Best Dishes:** Miso Black Cod, Spicy Beef Tenderloin, Soft Shell Crab Maki
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Located in The Galleria, offers a vibrant and lively atmosphere
- **Location:** The Galleria, Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi

Zuma Abu Dhabi, part of the renowned global chain, brings the authentic Japanese izakaya experience to the capital. Known for its stylish decor and innovative dishes, it's a great choice for a sophisticated night out.

**Tip:** Zuma's weekend brunch is legendary, offering a wide range of dishes and beverages in a lively setting.

### 6. LPM Restaurant & Bar

- **Cuisine Type:** French Mediterranean
- **Price Range:** AED 450-650 per person (Approx. $122-$177)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Essential for weekends and special occasions
- **Best Dishes:** Burrata, Grilled Lamb Cutlets, Crème Brûlée
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Offers a bright and airy atmosphere with a subtle Mediterranean touch
- **Location:** The Galleria, Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi

LPM Restaurant & Bar is the epitome of French elegance fused with Mediterranean flavors. This restaurant is perfect for romantic dinners and celebratory occasions.

**Insider Tip:** Pair your meal with their carefully curated wine list for an enhanced dining experience.

## Unique Dining Concepts

### 7. COYA Abu Dhabi

- **Cuisine Type:** Peruvian
- **Price Range:** AED 350-550 per person (Approx. $95-$150)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Reservations are recommended to secure a table in the main dining area
- **Best Dishes:** Ceviche, Anticuchos, Arroz Nikkei
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Offers a vibrant and colorful setting with traditional Peruvian art
- **Location:** The Galleria, Al Maryah Island, Abu Dhabi

COYA Abu Dhabi brings the flavors of Peru to the UAE with a contemporary twist, offering a lively atmosphere that is perfect for both casual and special occasions.

**Tip:** Don't miss their Pisco Sour, a classic Peruvian cocktail that perfectly complements the menu.

### 8. Catch at St. Regis

- **Cuisine Type:** Seafood
- **Price Range:** AED 300-500 per person (Approx. $82-$136)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Early booking is advised, especially for outdoor seating
- **Best Dishes:** Lobster Thermidor, Grilled Octopus, Seafood Tower
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Located on the beach, offering stunning views of the Arabian Gulf
- **Location:** St. Regis Abu Dhabi, Nation Riviera Beach Club

Catch at St. Regis is a seafood lover's paradise, combining fresh catches with elegant beachfront dining. The relaxed yet sophisticated setting is ideal for both intimate dinners and larger gatherings.

**Insider Tip:** Opt for a table on the terrace to enjoy the sea breeze and spectacular sunset views.

## Classic Elegance

### 9. Bord Eau

- **Cuisine Type:** French
- **Price Range:** AED 600-800 per person (Approx. $163-$218)
- **Dress Code:** Formal
- **Reservation Tips:** Highly recommended due to limited seating
- **Best Dishes:** Foie Gras, Lobster Bisque, Duck Confit
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Offers a refined and intimate setting with classic French decor
- **Location:** Shangri-La Hotel, Qaryat Al Beri, Abu Dhabi

Bord Eau is a quintessential French dining experience, renowned for its classic dishes and impeccable service. This is the perfect venue for anniversaries or other significant celebrations.

**Tip:** Save room for dessert, as their selection of French pastries is not to be missed.

### 10. Villa Toscana

- **Cuisine Type:** Italian
- **Price Range:** AED 300-450 per person (Approx. $82-$122)
- **Dress Code:** Smart Casual
- **Reservation Tips:** Reservations are advised, especially for weekends
- **Best Dishes:** Risotto alla Milanese, Osso Buco, Tiramisu
- **Views/Atmosphere:** Offers a warm and inviting Tuscan-style atmosphere
- **Location:** St. Regis Abu Dhabi, Nation Towers

Villa Toscana transports diners to the heart of Italy with its authentic Tuscan flavors and rustic decor. It's an excellent choice for family celebrations and intimate dinners.

**Insider Tip:** Pair your meal with a selection from their extensive Italian wine list for a true Tuscan experience.

## Conclusion

Abu Dhabi's dining scene is as diverse and dynamic as the city itself, offering a range of exquisite experiences from Michelin-starred elegance to rooftop relaxation. Whether you're marking a special occasion or simply seeking a memorable meal, these restaurants promise to deliver an unforgettable culinary journey.
