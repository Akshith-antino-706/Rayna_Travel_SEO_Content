# An Unforgettable 2-Day Itinerary in Abu Dhabi, UAE

Explore the vibrant and modern city of Abu Dhabi, the capital of the United Arab Emirates, with this detailed 2-day itinerary. From iconic highlights to cultural gems, discover the best of what this fascinating city has to offer.

## Day 1: Iconic Highlights of Abu Dhabi

### Morning: Discover the Grand Mosque

**8:00 AM - 10:30 AM: Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque**

- **Address:** Sheikh Rashid Bin Saeed Street, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** Free
- **Opening Hours:** Saturday to Thursday 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM; Friday 4:30 PM - 10:00 PM
- **Travel Time:** 20 minutes by taxi from central Abu Dhabi (approx. 40 AED / $11 USD)

Begin your day with a visit to the awe-inspiring Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, one of the largest mosques in the world. This architectural marvel can accommodate up to 41,000 worshippers and is renowned for its stunning white marble domes, intricate floral designs, and the world's largest hand-knotted carpet.

**Tip:** Dress modestly; women should cover their heads, and both men and women should wear loose-fitting clothing that covers arms and legs. Abayas and kanduras are available for free at the mosque entrance.

### Late Morning: Explore the Presidential Palace

**11:00 AM - 1:00 PM: Qasr Al Watan**

- **Address:** Al Ras Al Akhdar, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** 65 AED (approx. $18 USD) for adults
- **Opening Hours:** Daily 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- **Travel Time:** 15 minutes by taxi from the Grand Mosque (approx. 30 AED / $8 USD)

Head to Qasr Al Watan, the Presidential Palace, to delve into the UAE's rich heritage. This stunning palace offers insights into the country's governance and culture through its grand halls, libraries, and exhibitions.

**Insider Tip:** Don't miss the Palace in Motion light and sound show in the evening if you have time to return. It's a spectacular experience that brings the palace's story to life.

### Lunch: Dine with a View

**1:15 PM - 2:30 PM: Observation Deck at 300**

- **Location:** Jumeirah at Etihad Towers, Level 74
- **Cost:** 95 AED (approx. $26 USD) including 50 AED credit for food and beverages
- **Travel Time:** 10 minutes by taxi from Qasr Al Watan (approx. 20 AED / $5 USD)

Enjoy lunch at the Observation Deck at 300, offering panoramic views of Abu Dhabi’s skyline. Indulge in light bites and refreshments while taking in the breathtaking scenery.

**Tip:** Reservations are recommended, especially for window seats.

### Afternoon: Visit the Louvre Abu Dhabi

**3:00 PM - 5:30 PM: Louvre Abu Dhabi**

- **Address:** Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** 63 AED (approx. $17 USD) for adults
- **Opening Hours:** Tuesday to Sunday 10:00 AM - 6:30 PM; closed on Mondays
- **Travel Time:** 15 minutes by taxi from Jumeirah at Etihad Towers (approx. 30 AED / $8 USD)

Explore the Louvre Abu Dhabi, a universal museum that bridges Eastern and Western art. The impressive dome structure and curated collections make this an intellectual and visual delight.

**Insider Tip:** Download the Louvre Abu Dhabi app for an enhanced audio-guided tour.

### Evening: Relax at the Corniche

**6:00 PM onwards: Abu Dhabi Corniche**

- **Location:** Corniche Road, Abu Dhabi
- **Cost:** Free
- **Travel Time:** 15 minutes by taxi from the Louvre (approx. 30 AED / $8 USD)

Conclude your day with a leisurely stroll along the Abu Dhabi Corniche, a beautiful waterfront promenade. Enjoy the sunset, people-watch, or rent a bike to explore the area.

**Dinner Recommendation:** Head to Nation Riviera Beach Club for a seafood dinner at Catch, a beachside restaurant with a relaxed ambiance.

## Day 2: Cultural Immersion and Exploration

### Morning: Delve into Heritage

**8:30 AM - 10:00 AM: Heritage Village**

- **Address:** Marina Mall, Corniche Breakwater, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** Free
- **Opening Hours:** Saturday to Thursday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM; Friday 3:30 PM - 9:00 PM
- **Travel Time:** 10 minutes by taxi from central Abu Dhabi (approx. 25 AED / $7 USD)

Start your day at the Heritage Village to experience traditional Emirati life. Explore Bedouin tents, workshops, and an open museum that showcases the UAE's past.

**Tip:** Visit the small souk inside the village for unique handmade crafts and souvenirs.

### Late Morning: Step into History

**10:30 AM - 12:00 PM: Qasr Al Hosn**

- **Address:** Rashid Bin Saeed Al Maktoum Street, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** 30 AED (approx. $8 USD) for adults
- **Opening Hours:** Saturday to Thursday 10:00 AM - 9:00 PM; Friday 2:00 PM - 10:00 PM
- **Travel Time:** 10 minutes by taxi from Heritage Village (approx. 20 AED / $5 USD)

Visit Qasr Al Hosn, the oldest stone building in Abu Dhabi, which has served as a fortress, a seat of government, and a residence for the ruling family. The exhibitions here offer a glimpse into the city's development over the centuries.

**Insider Tip:** Participate in the guided tours for in-depth stories about the fort's history.

### Lunch: Savor Local Flavors

**12:15 PM - 1:30 PM: Al Fanar Restaurant & Café**

- **Location:** The Venetian Village, The Ritz-Carlton Abu Dhabi
- **Cost:** Approx. 100 AED per person (approx. $27 USD)
- **Travel Time:** 15 minutes by taxi from Qasr Al Hosn (approx. 30 AED / $8 USD)

Experience authentic Emirati cuisine at Al Fanar Restaurant & Café. Try traditional dishes like machboos and harees in a setting that replicates the 1960s UAE.

**Tip:** Consider sampling the date pudding for dessert, a local favorite.

### Afternoon: Art and Innovation

**2:00 PM - 4:00 PM: Manarat Al Saadiyat**

- **Address:** Saadiyat Island, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** Free for general exhibitions
- **Opening Hours:** Daily 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- **Travel Time:** 20 minutes by taxi from The Venetian Village (approx. 40 AED / $11 USD)

Spend your afternoon exploring Manarat Al Saadiyat, a cultural center that hosts art exhibitions, workshops, and events. It is a hub for creativity and innovation in Abu Dhabi.

**Insider Tip:** Check the schedule for temporary exhibitions or workshops that might pique your interest.

### Evening: Sunset at Yas Island

**4:30 PM - 7:00 PM: Yas Marina Circuit**

- **Location:** Yas Island, Abu Dhabi
- **Entry Fee:** Varies by activity
- **Travel Time:** 30 minutes by taxi from Manarat Al Saadiyat (approx. 50 AED / $14 USD)

If you're a motorsport enthusiast, visit Yas Marina Circuit, home to the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix. Participate in a guided tour or, if timing permits, book a driving experience on the track.

**Dinner Recommendation:** Enjoy dinner at Cipriani Yas Island, known for its Italian cuisine and stunning views over the marina.

**7:30 PM onwards: Yas Bay Waterfront**

- **Location:** Yas Island, Abu Dhabi
- **Cost:** Free to explore
- **Travel Time:** 5 minutes by taxi from Yas Marina Circuit (approx. 15 AED / $4 USD)

End your day at Yas Bay Waterfront, a lively area with entertainment, dining, and beautiful views of the Arabian Gulf. It's perfect for a relaxing stroll or enjoying live music at one of the bars.

## Day 2 Alternatives

For travelers with different interests, consider these alternatives:

- **Adventure Seekers:** Spend the afternoon at Warner Bros. World Abu Dhabi (entry: 345 AED / $94 USD), a thrilling theme park with rides and attractions for all ages.
- **Nature Enthusiasts:** Visit the Mangrove National Park for kayaking and bird watching (guided tours start at 150 AED / $41 USD).
- **Shopping Lovers:** Explore Yas Mall, one of the largest shopping centers in Abu Dhabi, offering a wide range of international and local brands.

## Conclusion

Abu Dhabi is a city that seamlessly blends tradition with modernity. This 2-day itinerary highlights the best of the city’s iconic sites and cultural experiences. Whether you're marveling at architectural wonders or immersing yourself in the rich Emirati heritage, your journey through Abu Dhabi will be both enlightening and memorable. Enjoy your stay in this dynamic capital city!
