# The Best Combo Tours and Activity Passes in New York City

New York City is a bustling metropolis filled with iconic attractions, diverse neighborhoods, and endless entertainment options. For travelers looking to maximize their experience while saving money, combo tours and activity passes offer excellent solutions. In this article, we’ll explore the best options available, including multi-day passes, museum passes, attraction passes, and activity bundles. We’ll provide details on what's included, costs, where to book, and advice on which packages offer good value.

## 1. New York CityPASS

### Overview
The New York CityPASS is a popular choice for first-time visitors, allowing you to explore six major attractions at a discounted price.

### What's Included
- Admission to the **American Museum of Natural History**
- **The Metropolitan Museum of Art** (includes the Met Cloisters)
- **Top of the Rock Observation Deck** or **Empire State Building**
- **Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island** ferry tickets
- **9/11 Memorial & Museum** or **Intrepid Sea, Air & Space Museum**

### Duration
The pass is valid for **9 consecutive days** from the first use.

### Cost
- Adults: **$132**
- Children (ages 6-17): **$108**

### Where to Book
You can purchase the CityPASS online at the [official website](https://www.citypass.com/new-york).

### Savings
The CityPASS can save you up to **42%** compared to purchasing individual tickets.

### Booking Requirements
Reservations are not required for most attractions, but it's advisable to check for any specific requirements.

### Pass Validity and Transferability
Non-transferable. Valid for 9 days, starting from the first use.

## 2. New York Explorer Pass

### Overview
The New York Explorer Pass allows visitors to choose from over 90 attractions, providing flexibility and a personalized itinerary.

### What's Included
- Entry to **up to 10 attractions**, including museums, tours, and activities.
- Popular options include the **Statue of Liberty**, **Central Park Bike Rentals**, and the **One World Observatory**.

### Duration
Valid for **60 days** from the first use.

### Cost
- 3 attractions: **$99**
- 5 attractions: **$139**
- 7 attractions: **$179**
- 10 attractions: **$229**

### Where to Book
Available for purchase on the [official website](https://www.newyorkexplorerpass.com).

### Savings
Typically offers a savings of **up to 50%** compared to buying individual tickets.

### Booking Requirements
Reservations may be required for certain attractions, so check before your visit.

### Pass Validity and Transferability
Non-transferable. Valid for 60 days.

## 3. New York Museum Pass

### Overview
The New York Museum Pass is perfect for art lovers. This pass gives you access to a wide range of museums across the city.

### What's Included
- Admission to over **30 museums**, including the **Museum of Modern Art (MoMA)**, **Whitney Museum of American Art**, and **American Museum of Natural History**.

### Duration
Valid for **3 consecutive days**.

### Cost
- Adults: **$125**
- Children (ages 5-12): **$50**

### Where to Book
Purchase online at the [New York Museum Pass website](https://www.nymuseumpass.com).

### Savings
You can save about **30-40%** when visiting multiple museums compared to buying individual tickets.

### Booking Requirements
Reservations may be necessary for some museums, especially for special exhibitions.

### Pass Validity and Transferability
Non-transferable. Valid for 3 consecutive days.

## 4. The Sightseeing Pass

### Overview
The Sightseeing Pass offers unlimited access to over 100 attractions, tours, and activities, including hop-on-hop-off bus tours.

### What's Included
- Unlimited access to attractions and tours, including the **Statue of Liberty**, **Empire State Building**, and various walking tours.
- Optional **Hop-On Hop-Off Bus Tour**.

### Duration
Available in **1, 2, 3, 5, or 7-day** options.

### Cost
- 1-Day: **$139**
- 2-Day: **$179**
- 3-Day: **$229**
- 5-Day: **$299**
- 7-Day: **$359**

### Where to Book
Book online at the [Sightseeing Pass website](https://www.sightseeingpass.com).

### Savings
Offers significant savings, especially for those planning to visit many attractions in a short time. Savings can be upwards of **50%**.

### Booking Requirements
No reservations are needed for most attractions, but check specific requirements for popular sites.

### Pass Validity and Transferability
Non-transferable. Valid for the duration purchased.

## 5. The Big Apple Pass

### Overview
The Big Apple Pass is a great all-in-one ticket for those who want to see the highlights of NYC without the commitment of multiple individual tickets.

### What's Included
- Access to **over 40 attractions**, including the **Empire State Building**, **Statue of Liberty**, and a variety of tours.

### Duration
Valid for **30 days** from the first use.

### Cost
- Adults: **$129**
- Children (ages 3-12): **$89**

### Where to Book
Available on the [Big Apple Pass website](https://www.bigapplepass.com).

### Savings
You can save up to **50%** compared to purchasing tickets separately.

### Booking Requirements
Reservations are generally not required, but check for specific attractions.

### Pass Validity and Transferability
Non-transferable. Valid for 30 days.

## Conclusion

When it comes to choosing a combo tour or activity pass in New York City, the best option depends on your interests and plans. Here’s a quick overview of which passes offer good value:

- **Best for First-Timers**: **New York CityPASS** – includes essential attractions.
- **Best for Flexibility**: **New York Explorer Pass** – choose your own adventure.
- **Art Lovers**: **New York Museum Pass** – access to many top museums.
- **Best All-in-One**: **The Sightseeing Pass** – unlimited access to attractions and tours.
- **Highlight Tour**: **The Big Apple Pass** – see the major sites without the hassle.

### Insider Tips
- Always check for any special promotions or discounts before purchasing.
- Consider the number of attractions you realistically plan to visit; sometimes, it's cheaper to buy tickets individually.
- Remember to read the fine print regarding exchange procedures and validity to avoid any misunderstandings.

With these passes, you can explore the best of New York City while keeping your budget in check!
