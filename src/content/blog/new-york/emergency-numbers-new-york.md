# Essential Emergency Contact Numbers for New York City

### What to Say When You Call

When calling any of these numbers, be clear and concise. If you are speaking English, you can simply say:

- **For Emergency Services**: "I need help; there’s an emergency at [your location]."
- **For Poison Control**: "I believe someone has been poisoned; please advise."
- **For Hospitals/Urgent Care**: "I need to know if you can see a patient; what are your wait times?"
