# The Ultimate Guide to Istanbul's Nightlife and Evening Entertainment

Istanbul is not just a city rich in history and culture; it also boasts a vibrant nightlife scene that pulsates with energy well into the early hours. From rooftop bars with breathtaking views to exciting night markets, cultural performances, and late-night dining, there’s something for everyone. Here’s everything you need to know to make the most of your evenings in Istanbul.

## Best Neighborhoods for Nightlife

### 1. Taksim and Beyoğlu
This is the heart of Istanbul’s nightlife. Bustling with activity, you’ll find an array of bars, clubs, and restaurants along İstiklal Avenue, a pedestrian street filled with shops and street performers.

### 2. Kadıköy
On the Asian side of Istanbul, Kadıköy offers a more laid-back vibe with trendy bars and cafes, perfect for a casual night out.

### 3. Ortaköy
Known for its stunning views of the Bosphorus, Ortaköy has several upscale clubs and beachfront bars that come alive at night.

### 4. Karaköy
This neighborhood blends historical charm with modern establishments, offering unique bars and seafood restaurants along the waterfront.

## Bars and Clubs

### Rooftop Bars

#### 1. **Maya**
- **Location**: Karaköy
- **Atmosphere**: Chic and modern, with stunning views of the Golden Horn.
- **Opening Hours**: 5 PM - 1 AM
- **Dress Code**: Smart casual.
- **Price Range**: Cocktails starting at 100 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Arrive early to secure a good spot for sunset views.

#### 2. **360 Istanbul**
- **Location**: Taksim
- **Atmosphere**: Trendy and vibrant, offering panoramic views of the city.
- **Opening Hours**: 6 PM - 2 AM
- **Dress Code**: Upscale casual.
- **Price Range**: Cocktails around 120 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Their late-night DJ sessions attract a lively crowd, so plan to stay for the party.

### Beachfront Bars

#### 1. **The Marmara Beach Club**
- **Location**: Ortaköy
- **Atmosphere**: Relaxed and breezy, perfect for unwinding by the water.
- **Opening Hours**: 5 PM - 12 AM
- **Dress Code**: Casual beach attire.
- **Price Range**: Drinks around 80 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Try the local fish dishes for a memorable meal by the sea.

### Casual Bars

#### 1. **Sahrap**
- **Location**: Kadıköy
- **Atmosphere**: Cozy and friendly, ideal for a casual night out.
- **Opening Hours**: 4 PM - 12 AM
- **Dress Code**: Casual.
- **Price Range**: Beers around 50 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Check their schedule for live music nights featuring local artists.

### Upscale Clubs

#### 1. **Sortie**
- **Location**: Ortaköy
- **Atmosphere**: Luxurious and glamorous, attracting an upscale clientele.
- **Opening Hours**: 10 PM - 4 AM
- **Dress Code**: Chic and stylish.
- **Price Range**: Entry fee around 200 TL (includes one drink).
- **Insider Tip**: Book a table in advance for a more exclusive experience.

## Night Markets and Bazaars

### 1. **Kadıköy Market**
- **Location**: Kadıköy
- **Operating Hours**: 6 PM - 11 PM (Thursday to Saturday)
- **Atmosphere**: Lively and colorful, filled with stalls selling everything from food to crafts.
- **Tips**: Sample local street food like "simit" (sesame bread) and Turkish delight.

### 2. **Grand Bazaar Night Market**
- **Location**: Fatih
- **Operating Hours**: 8 PM - 12 AM (weekend)
- **Atmosphere**: Bustling and vibrant, perfect for late-night shopping.
- **Tips**: Bargaining is expected, so don’t hesitate to negotiate prices.

## Evening Cultural Shows and Performances

### 1. **Whirling Dervishes at Galata Mevlevi House**
- **Location**: Galata
- **Show Times**: 7 PM - 8 PM (daily)
- **Atmosphere**: Spiritual and mesmerizing, showcasing traditional Sufi dance.
- **Ticket Price**: Approximately 100 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Arrive early for the best seats.

### 2. **Turkish Night Show**
- **Location**: Various venues, including restaurants in Taksim and Sultanahmet.
- **Show Times**: Typically from 8 PM - 11 PM.
- **Atmosphere**: Festive and lively, featuring folk dances and music.
- **Ticket Price**: Dinner and show packages start at around 300 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Some venues offer free pick-up and drop-off services.

## Night Tours

### 1. **Bosphorus Night Cruise**
- **Starting Point**: Eminönü
- **Duration**: 2-3 hours
- **Atmosphere**: Romantic and scenic, perfect for couples.
- **Price**: Ranges from 150 TL to 400 TL, depending on the package.
- **Insider Tip**: Bring a light jacket; it can get chilly on the water.

### 2. **Historical Walking Tour**
- **Location**: Sultanahmet
- **Operating Hours**: 7 PM - 10 PM
- **Atmosphere**: Enchanting as landmarks are beautifully illuminated.
- **Price**: Approximately 100 TL per person.
- **Insider Tip**: Wear comfortable shoes; there’s a lot of walking involved.

## Late-Night Dining

### 1. **Çiya Sofrası**
- **Location**: Kadıköy
- **Operating Hours**: 10 AM - 1 AM
- **Atmosphere**: Authentic and cozy, serving traditional Turkish dishes.
- **Price Range**: Main dishes around 50-100 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Don’t miss their unique meze options.

### 2. **Zubeyir Ocakbasi**
- **Location**: Taksim
- **Operating Hours**: 5 PM - 1 AM
- **Atmosphere**: Lively with a local feel, specializing in grilled meats.
- **Price Range**: Main dishes around 80-150 TL.
- **Insider Tip**: Order the kebabs and enjoy them with a side of fresh bread.

## Nighttime Sightseeing

### 1. **Hagia Sophia**
- **Location**: Sultanahmet
- **Operating Hours**: 9 PM - 10 PM (night tours available)
- **Atmosphere**: Majestic and serene under the night sky.
