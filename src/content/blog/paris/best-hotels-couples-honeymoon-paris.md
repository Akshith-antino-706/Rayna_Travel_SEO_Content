# The Most Romantic Hotels in Paris for Couples and Honeymooners

Paris, often dubbed the "City of Love," is the perfect destination for couples and honeymooners. With its stunning architecture, enchanting streets, and world-class cuisine, the city offers a plethora of romantic experiences. Here’s a curated list of the most romantic hotels in Paris, ideal for couples looking to celebrate their love. 

## 1. Hôtel Plaza Athénée

### Romantic Setting
Located on the prestigious Avenue Montaigne, Hôtel Plaza Athénée boasts a classic Parisian charm with its stunning views of the Eiffel Tower. The hotel’s lush garden is an ideal spot for a romantic afternoon.

### Couples' Experiences
Guests can enjoy private tours of the Eiffel Tower or Seine River cruises arranged by the hotel. The concierge can also organize a bespoke picnic in the nearby Jardin des Tuileries.

### Spa Treatments
The Dior Spa at Plaza Athénée is perfect for couples looking to unwind together. Indulge in the "Dior Back to Basics" treatment for €500, which includes a 90-minute massage in a luxurious couples suite.

### Private Dining Options
Dine at the Michelin-starred restaurant, Alain Ducasse au Plaza Athénée, where you can enjoy a romantic meal featuring seasonal ingredients. The restaurant offers a private dining room for intimate dinners.

### Special Honeymoon Packages
The hotel offers a "Romantic Escape" package starting at €1,200 per night, which includes a bottle of champagne, daily breakfast, and a complimentary couples’ spa treatment.

## 2. Le Meurice

### Romantic Setting
Le Meurice, a palace hotel, combines classic French elegance with modern luxury. With its stunning views of the Tuileries Garden, it’s an ideal backdrop for romance.

### Couples' Experiences
Take part in a private culinary workshop with the hotel's chef or enjoy a personalized perfume-making experience at nearby fragrance boutiques.

### Spa Treatments
The spa offers a special "Couples Retreat" treatment for €350, allowing you to relax with a dual massage and skincare treatments in a serene environment.

### Private Dining Options
Le Meurice offers a unique dining experience with its Chef’s Table, where you can enjoy a custom menu prepared just for you. 

### Special Honeymoon Packages
Their "Romance in Paris" package starts at €1,000 per night and includes a candlelit dinner, champagne, and a bouquet of flowers upon arrival.

## 3. Hôtel de Crillon, A Rosewood Hotel

### Romantic Setting
With its striking 18th-century architecture, Hôtel de Crillon is located at Place de la Concorde, providing breathtaking views of the city. The hotel features a beautiful terrace perfect for sunset views.

### Couples' Experiences
The hotel can arrange private guided tours of the Louvre or a romantic Seine riverboat dinner cruise.

### Spa Treatments
The Sense Spa offers a "Couples Escape" package for €700, which includes a 90-minute treatment of your choice, followed by a private soak in a luxurious spa bath.

### Private Dining Options
Indulge in a private dining experience on the terrace with views of the Eiffel Tower. The chef will prepare a personalized menu based on your preferences.

### Special Honeymoon Packages
The "Honeymoon Bliss" package starts at €1,500 per night and includes complimentary champagne, a romantic dinner for two, and a couple’s spa treatment.

## 4. Hôtel des Grands Boulevards

### Romantic Setting
This trendy boutique hotel features a rooftop terrace with stunning views of Paris. The cozy interiors and unique decor create an intimate atmosphere.

### Couples' Experiences
Enjoy a romantic evening at the hotel’s rooftop bar, where you can sip cocktails while watching the sunset over the city.

### Spa Treatments
While the hotel doesn’t have an on-site spa, they partner with local wellness centers to offer couples’ massages starting at €120.

### Private Dining Options
Dine at the hotel’s restaurant, which serves a seasonal menu inspired by French cuisine. You can opt for a private dining experience in one of the hotel’s charming salons.

### Special Honeymoon Packages
The "Love Package" starts at €250 per night, including a bottle of champagne, breakfast in bed, and a romantic welcome gift.

## 5. Le Relais Saint-Honoré

### Romantic Setting
A charming boutique hotel in the heart of Paris, Le Relais Saint-Honoré is known for its intimate ambiance and proximity to iconic landmarks like the Louvre and Palais Royal.

### Couples' Experiences
Stroll hand-in-hand through the nearby Palais Royal gardens or take a romantic evening walk along the Seine.

### Spa Treatments
The hotel does not have an on-site spa but can arrange in-room couples massages from local providers for around €150.

### Private Dining Options
Enjoy a candlelit dinner at the hotel’s cozy bistro, where you can savor traditional French dishes.

### Special Honeymoon Packages
Their "Romantic Getaway" package starts at €180 per night and includes a complimentary bottle of wine and late checkout.

## 6. Hôtel du Petit Moulin

### Romantic Setting
This boutique hotel is located in the Marais district and features unique, artist-designed rooms that create a cozy and whimsical atmosphere.

### Couples' Experiences
Explore the charming Marais neighborhood with its cobblestone streets, boutiques, and galleries. The hotel can also arrange a private wine tasting experience.

### Spa Treatments
While there is no spa on-site, the hotel can help arrange a couples’ massage in the comfort of your room.

### Private Dining Options
Enjoy a meal at the hotel’s highly-rated restaurant, which offers authentic French cuisine in an intimate setting.

### Special Honeymoon Packages
The "Marais Romance" package starts at €220 per night and includes breakfast in bed, a bottle of champagne, and a personalized gift.

## Conclusion

Paris is undoubtedly a romantic destination, and choosing the right hotel can enhance your experience. Whether you prefer the opulence of a palace hotel or the charm of a boutique stay, there’s something for every couple in this enchanting city. From intimate dining to luxurious spa treatments, these hotels offer a variety of romantic experiences to make your stay unforgettable. Make sure to check for seasonal offers and book in advance to secure your dream getaway. Happy travels!
