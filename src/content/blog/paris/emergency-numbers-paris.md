# Essential Emergency Contact Numbers for Paris: A Traveler's Guide

Traveling to Paris, often referred to as the "City of Light," is an exciting adventure. However, it's crucial to be prepared for emergencies. Here, we’ve compiled a comprehensive list of essential emergency contact numbers, embassy details, major hospitals, and other helpful resources. This guide will ensure you can navigate any unexpected situations during your stay in Paris.

## Emergency Services in Paris

### Police
- **Emergency Number:** 17
- **Non-Emergency Police Contact:** +33 1 53 73 53 73 (English may be spoken)
  
### Ambulance
- **Emergency Number:** 15
- **Ambulance Service:** SAMU (Service d'Aide Médicale Urgente)
  
### Fire Department
- **Emergency Number:** 18
- **Paris Fire Brigade:** 18 (for fires, rescues, and other emergencies)

### Poison Control
- **Poison Control Center:** 0 800 590 900 
- **International Dialing:** +33 800 590 900 (toll-free within France)

### Tourist Police
- **Tourist Police Hotline:** +33 1 49 52 50 00
- **Location:** 4 Rue de la Montagne Sainte Geneviève, 75005 Paris
- **Note:** They are available to assist tourists with theft, emergencies, and general inquiries.

### General Emergency Information
In case of any emergency, dial 112, the European emergency number valid across the EU. This number will connect you to police, fire, and medical services.

## How to Make Calls in France

### International Dialing
To call from outside France, dial your country’s international access code (e.g., 011 from the U.S.) followed by the country code for France (+33) and then the local number (dropping the leading zero).

### Calling Within France
- To call within Paris, simply use the 10-digit number without any area codes.
- English is commonly spoken in tourist areas and establishments, but it’s always good to know basic French phrases.

### What to Say
When calling emergency services, clearly state:
1. **Your location** (address or landmark).
2. **Nature of the emergency** (medical, fire, theft, etc.).
3. **Your name** and **phone number** for follow-up.
