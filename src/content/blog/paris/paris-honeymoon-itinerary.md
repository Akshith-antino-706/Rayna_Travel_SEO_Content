# A Romantic Honeymoon Itinerary in Paris

Paris, the City of Love, is the perfect backdrop for a romantic honeymoon. With its charming streets, exquisite cuisine, and breathtaking views, your trip will be filled with intimate moments and unforgettable experiences. Below is a comprehensive 7-day itinerary that balances sightseeing, relaxation, and romance.

## Day 1: Arrival and Relaxation

### Afternoon: Arrival and Check-In
- **Accommodation**: For ultra-luxury, consider **Le Meurice** (from €800/night), or for something more mid-range, try **Hôtel Le Six** (from €200/night). Both offer romantic settings and excellent service.
- **Transportation**: Pre-book a private transfer from the airport through **Blacklane** (from €80 for a sedan) for a stress-free start.

### Evening: Seine River Dinner Cruise
- Enjoy a romantic dinner cruise on the Seine with **Bateaux Parisiens**. Prices start at €85 per person and include a gourmet 3-course meal.
- **Insider Tip**: Book your cruise for sunset to enjoy the stunning views of the Eiffel Tower lighting up as night falls.

## Day 2: Exploring Iconic Landmarks

### Morning: A Stroll through Montmartre
- Visit the iconic **Sacré-Cœur Basilica** and enjoy panoramic views of the city. 
- Stop by **Le Consulat**, a charming café for a breakfast of croissants and café au lait.

### Afternoon: Private Tour of the Louvre
- Book a private guided tour of the **Louvre** (prices from €300 for a couple). Skip the lines and get an insider's perspective on the artwork.
- **Lunch**: Enjoy a light lunch at **Le Fumoir**, near the Louvre, known for its intimate atmosphere.

### Evening: Romantic Dinner at Le Jules Verne
- Reserve a table at **Le Jules Verne**, located inside the Eiffel Tower. Prices range from €105 to €350 per person depending on the menu.
- **Insider Tip**: Request a window seat for breathtaking views of the Paris skyline.

## Day 3: Day Trip to Champagne

### Morning: Private Champagne Tour
- Book a private tour to the Champagne region with **A La Française** (from €195 per person). Visit family-owned vineyards and enjoy tastings.
  
### Afternoon: Lunch in Épernay
- Enjoy lunch at **La Grillade Gourmande** in Épernay (around €40 per person), known for its local cuisine and cozy ambiance.

### Evening: Return to Paris for a Relaxing Spa Treatment
- Indulge in a couples spa treatment at **Spa de La Réserve** (from €250 for two). Opt for their signature massage followed by a relaxation session.

## Day 4: Art and Gardens

### Morning: Musee d'Orsay
- Visit the **Musée d'Orsay** to admire impressionist masterpieces. Pre-book tickets to avoid long lines (€16 per person).
  
### Afternoon: Stroll Through the Luxembourg Gardens
- Enjoy a leisurely walk in the **Jardin du Luxembourg**. Pack a picnic with goodies from **Pierre Hermé**, a renowned patisserie.

### Evening: Wine Tasting Experience
- Join a wine tasting at **Ô Chateau** (approximately €80 per person). Learn about French wines while sampling exquisite selections.

## Day 5: Romance at Versailles

### Morning: Visit Palace of Versailles
- Book a private tour of **Versailles** (prices from €400 for a couple). Explore the palace's opulence and beautiful gardens without the crowds.
  
### Afternoon: Lunch at La Petite Venise
- Dine at **La Petite Venise** within the gardens (around €30 per person). Enjoy traditional French cuisine in a romantic setting.

### Evening: Return to Paris for a Seine River Stroll
- Take a romantic evening stroll along the Seine, stopping at **Pont Alexandre III** for stunning views and photo opportunities.

## Day 6: Intimate Experiences and Culture

### Morning: Cooking Class
- Participate in a couples cooking class with **Le Foodist** (from €200 for two). Create your own French dishes and enjoy your culinary creations.

### Afternoon: Afternoon Tea at the Ritz
- Experience a lavish afternoon tea at the **Ritz Paris** (around €75 per person). The opulent setting adds to the romance.

### Evening: Cabaret Show at Moulin Rouge
- Book tickets for a show at the **Moulin Rouge** (from €100 per person). Enjoy a classic Parisian night filled with vibrant performances.

## Day 7: Farewell with a Bang

### Morning: Relaxing Morning
- Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at your hotel. Consider a late checkout to savor your last moments in Paris.

### Afternoon: Private Boat Ride
- Rent a private boat for a scenic ride on the Canal Saint-Martin (from €150 for an hour). Bring along a picnic to enjoy on board.

### Evening: Proposal or Vow Renewal
- Arrange a surprise proposal or vow renewal at the **Jardin des Tuileries** or under the sparkling lights of the Eiffel Tower. Consider hiring a photographer to capture the moment.

### Final Dinner: Michelin-Star Dining
- For your final night, indulge in a Michelin-starred experience at **L'Arpège** (from €180 per person). Celebrate your love with exquisite cuisine.

## Budget Considerations

### Budget-Friendly: 
- Accommodation: **Hôtel de la Porte Dorée** (€100/night)
- Dining: Casual bistros and cafes (€15-€30 per meal)
- Activities: Museums and parks (most under €20)

### Mid-Range: 
- Accommodation: **Hôtel Le Six** (€200/night)
- Dining: Brasseries and wine bars (€40-€70 per meal)
- Activities: Private tours and cooking classes (€100-€200 per person)

### Ultra-Luxury: 
- Accommodation: **Le Meurice** (€800/night)
- Dining: High-end restaurants (€100-€500 per meal)
- Activities: Exclusive private tours and spa treatments (€250+ each)

## Surprise Ideas and Tips for a Memorable Trip
- Arrange for flowers to be delivered to your hotel upon arrival.
- Consider scheduling a professional photoshoot for a romantic session in iconic locations.
- Book experiences in advance to secure the best times and spots.
- Take time each day to relax and soak in the Parisian atmosphere—find a café and simply enjoy the moment together.

With this romantic itinerary, you will create lasting memories that celebrate your love in one of the most beautiful cities in the world. Enjoy your honeymoon in Paris!
