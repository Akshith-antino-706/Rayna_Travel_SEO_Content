# Romantic Honeymoon Packages in Paris: Your Ultimate Guide

Paris, often dubbed the “City of Love,” is a dream destination for honeymooners. With its enchanting streets, stunning architecture, and world-renowned cuisine, there's no shortage of romantic experiences to be had. This guide will help you navigate through some of the best honeymoon packages available, focusing on luxury accommodations, romantic activities, special meals, spa treatments, and couple experiences.

## Luxurious Accommodations

### 1. **Le Meurice**
- **Pricing:** Starting at €1,200 per night
- **Highlights:** This opulent hotel offers stunning views of the Tuileries Garden and is located close to the Louvre. Rooms can be elegantly decorated for honeymooners, complete with rose petals and champagne upon arrival.

### 2. **Shangri-La Hotel Paris**
- **Pricing:** Starting at €1,300 per night
- **Highlights:** Situated in a former royal residence, the Shangri-La offers breathtaking views of the Eiffel Tower. Honeymoon packages often include a complimentary breakfast and a private dinner for two on the terrace.

### 3. **Le Bristol Paris**
- **Pricing:** Starting at €1,100 per night
- **Highlights:** Known for its lavish interiors and impeccable service, Le Bristol features a rooftop swimming pool and a luxurious spa. Honeymooners can indulge in a romantic room setup with personalized touches.

## Romantic Activities

### 1. **Seine River Dinner Cruise**
- **Pricing:** Approximately €100 per person
- **Details:** Enjoy a gourmet dinner while gliding past Paris’s iconic landmarks illuminated at night. Companies like Bateaux Parisiens offer a romantic atmosphere with live music.

### 2. **Hot Air Balloon Ride**
- **Pricing:** Around €30 per person
- **Details:** Take to the skies for a breathtaking view of Paris. Companies like Parc André Citroën offer sunrise and sunset rides, perfect for a romantic experience.

### 3. **Private Guided Tour of Paris**
- **Pricing:** Starting at €300 for a 3-hour tour
- **Details:** Book a private guide to take you through the hidden gems of the city. Tailor your itinerary to include your favorite sights or lesser-known locations.

## Special Meals

### 1. **Michelin-Star Dining**
- **Restaurants:** 
  - **Le Meurice Alain Ducasse** - Tasting menu starting at €380 per person.
  - **L’Arpège** - Known for its vegetable-focused menu, with a prix-fixe option around €300.

### 2. **Picnic in the Park**
- **Pricing:** €50 for a prepared basket
- **Details:** Many local shops and gourmet delis, like La Grande Épicerie, offer picnic baskets filled with French delicacies. Enjoy a romantic meal in the Luxembourg Gardens or along the Seine.

## Spa Treatments

### 1. **Spa at the Four Seasons Hotel George V**
- **Pricing:** Treatments starting at €200
- **Details:** Indulge in a couple’s massage or a signature facial. The spa offers a luxurious ambiance, perfect for relaxation.

### 2. **Hôtel de Crillon Spa**
- **Pricing:** Treatments starting at €150
- **Details:** Experience opulent treatments designed for couples. The spa often includes wellness packages that combine treatments with access to the hotel’s facilities.

## Couple Experiences

### 1. **Wine Tasting Tour**
- **Pricing:** Approximately €150 per person
- **Details:** Join a guided wine tasting in the Saint-Germain-des-Prés area, sampling some of the finest French wines. Operators like O Chateau offer intimate settings perfect for couples.

### 2. **Cooking Class**
- **Pricing:** Starting at €120 per person
- **Details:** Take a French cooking class together, learning to make classic dishes or pastries. Companies like La Cuisine Paris provide hands-on learning experiences.

## Available Packages

### All-Inclusive Packages
All-inclusive honeymoon packages typically cover accommodation, meals, activities, and sometimes even airport transfers. For example, **Luxury Escapes** offers a week-long package at Le Meurice for approximately €10,000, which includes spa treatments, romantic dinners, and guided tours.

### Partial Packages
Partial packages allow honeymooners to customize their experiences. You can choose specific activities or dining experiences while handling accommodation separately. Websites like **Viator** or **GetYourGuide** provide options for individual bookings.

### Customization Options
Many hotels and tour operators allow for customization. You can add special requests like room decorations, anniversary cakes, or surprise arrangements by contacting the hotel or tour operator directly when booking.

## Tips for Selecting Your Honeymoon Package

1. **Set a Budget:** Determine how much you'd like to spend, factoring in accommodations, meals, and activities. There are options for every price range, from budget-friendly to ultra-luxurious.

2. **Research Reviews:** Check reviews on websites like TripAdvisor to ensure the quality of the accommodations and experiences you are considering.

3. **Consider Timing:** The best time for a honeymoon in Paris is typically from mid-April to June and September to October when the weather is mild, and the crowds are smaller.

4. **Personalize Your Experience:** Don’t hesitate to ask for customizations based on your preferences. Many operators and hotels are willing to accommodate special requests.

5. **Book in Advance:** Popular hotels and experiences can fill up quickly, especially during peak tourist seasons. Book early to secure the best options.

## Making Your Honeymoon Extra Special

- **Surprise Elements:** Plan a surprise dinner or gift for your partner, such as a personalized photo book of your journey together.
- **Capture the Moments:** Hire a local photographer to capture candid moments around the city. Services like Flytographer offer professional photography sessions.
- **Stay Flexible:** While planning is essential, leaving some room for spontaneity can lead to unforgettable experiences.

### Conclusion

Paris offers a wealth of options for honeymooners, from lavish accommodations and gourmet dining to unforgettable experiences. Whether you opt for an all-inclusive package or a more tailored approach, the magic of the City of Love is sure to create lasting memories. By considering your budget, preferences, and special requests, you can curate the perfect romantic getaway that you and your partner will cherish forever.
