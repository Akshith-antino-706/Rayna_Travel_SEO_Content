# The Ultimate Guide to Paris's Best Restaurants: Cuisines, Price Points, and Atmospheres

Paris is a culinary paradise, offering a plethora of dining experiences from local bistros to Michelin-starred establishments. This guide will take you through the best restaurants across various cuisines and price points, ensuring you find the perfect dining spot for any occasion.

## French Cuisine

### 1. **Le Relais de l'Entrecôte**
- **Cuisine Type:** Classic French
- **Signature Dishes:** Steak frites, their secret sauce
- **Ambiance:** Cozy, bustling bistro
- **Price Range:** €25-€45
- **Location:** Multiple locations, with the original at 15 Rue Marbeuf, 75008 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Reservations recommended, especially for dinner
- **Noteworthy:** Famous for its steak frites served with a unique sauce. It’s a must-try for meat lovers!

### 2. **Chez Janou**
- **Cuisine Type:** Provençal
- **Signature Dishes:** Ratatouille, chocolate mousse
- **Ambiance:** Charming, rustic with a lovely courtyard
- **Price Range:** €30-€60
- **Location:** 2 Rue Roger Verlomme, 75003 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Recommended for dinner
- **Noteworthy:** Offers a delightful outdoor terrace filled with Mediterranean herbs. Try their homemade olive oil!

### 3. **Le Meurice**
- **Cuisine Type:** Michelin-Starred French
- **Signature Dishes:** Pigeon with beetroot, chocolate soufflé
- **Ambiance:** Luxurious and elegant
- **Price Range:** €100-€300
- **Location:** 228 Rue de Rivoli, 75001 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Essential, especially for the tasting menu
- **Noteworthy:** A true fine dining experience with a stunning view of the Tuileries Garden.

## Italian Cuisine

### 4. **Osteria Ferrara**
- **Cuisine Type:** Italian
- **Signature Dishes:** Homemade pasta, tiramisu
- **Ambiance:** Warm and inviting
- **Price Range:** €20-€50
- **Location:** 5 Rue de la Grange aux Belles, 75010 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Reservations are advisable
- **Noteworthy:** Known for its authentic Italian dishes and friendly service. Great for families!

### 5. **Pizza Chic**
- **Cuisine Type:** Italian (Pizza)
- **Signature Dishes:** Margherita, truffle pizza
- **Ambiance:** Casual, laid-back
- **Price Range:** €10-€25
- **Location:** 30 Rue de la Rèpublique, 75011 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Walk-ins welcome, but reservations for larger groups
- **Noteworthy:** Offers some of the best Neapolitan-style pizzas in Paris. Perfect for a quick meal!

## Asian Cuisine

### 6. **Le Grand Restaurant**
- **Cuisine Type:** Asian Fusion
- **Signature Dishes:** Lobster with lemongrass, miso-glazed eggplant
- **Ambiance:** Sleek, modern decor
- **Price Range:** €50-€150
- **Location:** 7 Rue de la Tremoille, 75016 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Essential, especially for dinner
- **Noteworthy:** Michelin-starred, this restaurant combines French techniques with Asian flavors for a unique dining experience.

### 7. **Shangri-La Hotel - Shang Palace**
- **Cuisine Type:** Cantonese
- **Signature Dishes:** Dim sum, Peking duck
- **Ambiance:** Elegant, luxurious
- **Price Range:** €80-€200
- **Location:** 10 Avenue d'Iéna, 75116 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Highly recommended
- **Noteworthy:** The only Michelin-starred Chinese restaurant in France, renowned for its exquisite dishes and stunning views of the Eiffel Tower.

## Middle Eastern Cuisine

### 8. **L’As du Fallafel**
- **Cuisine Type:** Middle Eastern
- **Signature Dishes:** Falafel sandwich, shawarma
- **Ambiance:** Casual, vibrant
- **Price Range:** €10-€20
- **Location:** 32 Rue des Rosiers, 75004 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** No reservations; first-come, first-served
- **Noteworthy:** Often cited as the best falafel in Paris. Perfect for a quick bite while exploring Le Marais.

### 9. **Chez Omar**
- **Cuisine Type:** Moroccan
- **Signature Dishes:** Couscous, tagine
- **Ambiance:** Colorful and lively
- **Price Range:** €20-€40
- **Location:** 47 Rue de Bretagne, 75003 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Recommended, especially on weekends
- **Noteworthy:** Known for its generous portions and welcoming atmosphere. A great place for group dining!

## American Cuisine

### 10. **Breakfast in America**
- **Cuisine Type:** American Diner
- **Signature Dishes:** Pancakes, burgers, milkshakes
- **Ambiance:** Retro diner vibe
- **Price Range:** €10-€25
- **Location:** 17 Rue des Ecoles, 75005 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** No reservations, walk-ins only
- **Noteworthy:** A slice of Americana in Paris, perfect for brunch or a casual meal.

## Contemporary Cuisine

### 11. **Le Cinq**
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary French
- **Signature Dishes:** Seasonal tasting menu, lobster with saffron
- **Ambiance:** Opulent and sophisticated
- **Price Range:** €150-€400
- **Location:** 31 Avenue George V, 75008 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Essential
- **Noteworthy:** Three Michelin stars and an elegant dining experience with impeccable service.

### 12. **Le Mary Celeste**
- **Cuisine Type:** Contemporary
- **Signature Dishes:** Avocado toast, seafood boards
- **Ambiance:** Trendy, hip atmosphere
- **Price Range:** €15-€35
- **Location:** 1 Rue Commines, 75003 Paris
- **Reservation Info:** Recommended for dinner
- **Noteworthy:** Known for its innovative cocktails and small plates, perfect for sharing.

## Tips for Dining in Paris

1. **Reservations:** Always make reservations for popular restaurants, especially for dinner on weekends. Use platforms like TheFork or directly through the restaurant’s website.
2. **Dining Hours:** Most Parisian restaurants serve lunch from 12 PM to 2:30 PM and dinner from 7 PM to 10:30 PM. Many close on Sundays or have reduced hours.
3. **Dress Codes:** While casual attire is acceptable in many bistros, upscale restaurants often require smart casual or formal wear. Check ahead to avoid any surprises.
4. **Payment Methods:** Credit cards are widely accepted, but it’s useful to carry some cash for smaller establishments. Look out for places that accept American Express, as they are less common.
