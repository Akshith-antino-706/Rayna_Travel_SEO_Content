# The Ultimate Guide to Singapore's Nightlife and Evening Entertainment

Singapore is a vibrant city that truly comes alive after sunset. With a multitude of bars, clubs, night markets, and cultural shows, there's something for everyone, whether you're a solo traveler, a family, or a group of friends. This guide will take you through the best spots in Singapore's nightlife, providing practical tips, operating hours, dress codes, and insider advice to ensure you have an unforgettable evening.

## Best Neighborhoods for Nightlife

- **Clarke Quay**: A bustling riverside hub known for its vibrant nightlife, featuring numerous bars and clubs.
- **Marina Bay**: Famous for its luxury rooftop bars and stunning city views, ideal for upscale dining and entertainment.
- **Orchard Road**: The shopping district transforms into a lively nightlife scene with casual bars and cafes.
- **Chinatown**: Offers a blend of traditional night markets and trendy bars, perfect for a more local experience.
- **Bugis**: Known for its eclectic mix of street food and casual dining options, along with vibrant night bazaars.

## Bars and Clubs

### Rooftop Bars

#### 1. **Marina Bay Sands SkyPark**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 11 AM - 10 PM
- **Dress Code**: Smart casual
- **Cost Range**: S$20 - S$40 for cocktails
- **Atmosphere**: Offers breathtaking views of the Singapore skyline. The ambiance is upscale and perfect for romantic evenings or special occasions.

#### 2. **Ce La Vi**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 5 PM - 2 AM
- **Dress Code**: Smart elegant
- **Cost Range**: S$18 - S$30 for drinks
- **Atmosphere**: Located atop the Marina Bay Sands, it features stunning views and a chic vibe, ideal for a night out with friends.

### Beachfront Bars

#### 3. **Tanjong Beach Club**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 10 AM - 10 PM
- **Dress Code**: Casual beachwear
- **Cost Range**: S$15 - S$25 for cocktails
- **Atmosphere**: A laid-back beachfront bar on Sentosa Island, perfect for sundowners and enjoying the sea breeze.

### Upscale Clubs

#### 5. **Zouk**
- **Operating Hours**: Wednesday to Saturday, 10 PM - 6 AM
- **Dress Code**: Smart casual
- **Cost Range**: S$30 - S$50 cover charge
- **Atmosphere**: One of Singapore's most iconic nightclubs, known for its world-class DJs and high-energy atmosphere.

## Night Markets and Bazaars

### 6. **Lau Pa Sat**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 24 hours
- **Cost Range**: S$5 - S$15 for meals
- **Atmosphere**: A historic hawker center offering a variety of local dishes. The night market vibe is vibrant, with an array of food stalls and local delicacies.

### 7. **Bugis Street Market**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 11 AM - 10 PM
- **Cost Range**: S$1 - S$10 for snacks
- **Atmosphere**: A bustling bazaar filled with street food, clothing, and accessories. An excellent place for late-night shopping and snacking.

## Evening Cultural Shows and Performances

### 8. **Gardens by the Bay Light Show**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 7:45 PM & 8:45 PM
- **Cost Range**: Free
- **Atmosphere**: A mesmerizing light and sound show featuring the iconic Supertree Grove, perfect for families and couples.

### 9. **Kampong Glam Cultural Tour**
- **Operating Hours**: Various tours available at night
- **Cost Range**: S$25 - S$50
- **Atmosphere**: Enjoy a guided tour through the historic Malay district, learning about the culture and heritage of Singapore.

## Night Tours

### 10. **Night Safari**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 7:15 PM - 12 AM
- **Cost Range**: S$45 for adults, S$30 for children
- **Atmosphere**: A unique wildlife park that operates at night, featuring tram rides and walking trails through different animal habitats. Great for families and wildlife enthusiasts.

## Late-Night Dining

### 11. **Chatterbox**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM; 6 PM - 10 PM
- **Cost Range**: S$30 - S$60 per person
- **Atmosphere**: An upscale restaurant known for its award-winning Mandarin chicken, perfect for a late dinner in a sophisticated setting.

### 12. **Supper at Newton Food Centre**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 24 hours
- **Cost Range**: S$5 - S$20
- **Atmosphere**: A popular spot for late-night local dishes, including seafood and satay, frequented by locals and tourists alike.

## Nighttime Sightseeing

### 13. **Marina Bay Sands Promenade**
- **Operating Hours**: Open 24 hours
- **Cost Range**: Free
- **Atmosphere**: A picturesque spot for a night stroll with stunning views of the Marina Bay skyline and the iconic Merlion.

### 14. **Singapore Flyer**
- **Operating Hours**: Daily, 3 PM - 10 PM
- **Cost Range**: S$33 for adults, S$21 for children
- **Atmosphere**: Experience breathtaking views of the city from the giant Ferris wheel, particularly beautiful at night when the city is illuminated.

## Safety Tips for Night Travel

- **Stay in Well-Lit Areas**: Stick to populated areas and avoid dark alleys or isolated places.
- **Use Licensed Taxis or Rideshares**: Opt for reputable taxi services or rideshare apps like Grab to ensure safe transportation.
- **Keep Your Belongings Secure**: Be mindful of pickpockets in crowded areas, especially in night markets.
- **Travel in Groups**: If possible, travel with friends or family, especially when walking back to your accommodation late at night.
