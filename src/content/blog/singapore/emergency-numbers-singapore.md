# Essential Emergency Contact Numbers for Singapore

Traveling to Singapore can be an exciting experience filled with vibrant culture, delicious food, and stunning sights. However, it's essential to be prepared for any emergencies that may arise during your stay. This guide compiles crucial emergency contact numbers, embassy and consulate information, major hospitals, and non-emergency numbers to ensure your trip is both enjoyable and safe.

## Emergency Services in Singapore

### Emergency Numbers

In Singapore, emergency services can be contacted using the following numbers:

- **Police:** 999
- **Ambulance:** 995
- **Fire Department:** 995
- **Poison Control:** 995 (for emergencies) or you can call the Singapore Poison Centre at +65 6222 6969.
- **Civil Defence (for fire and rescue services):** 995

### Important Notes:
- **Language:** English is widely spoken in Singapore, making it easier for travelers to communicate during emergencies.
- **What to Say:** When calling any of these numbers, state your emergency clearly and provide your location. For example, "I need an ambulance at [your location]."

## Tourist Police and Helplines

For tourists, Singapore offers specific services to help ensure your safety:

- **Tourist Police Hotline:** +65 6435 0000
- **Tourist Information:** You can also contact the Singapore Tourism Board at +65 6736 6622 for travel-related queries.
