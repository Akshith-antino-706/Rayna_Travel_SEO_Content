# Singapore's Best Hotels Across All Price Categories

Singapore is a bustling metropolis known for its blend of cultures, stunning architecture, and world-class amenities. With a diverse range of accommodations from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious five-star hotels, there's something for every traveler. Below is a comprehensive guide to the best hotels in Singapore, organized by price tier and neighborhood.

## Budget-Friendly Hotels

### 1. **Hotel G Singapore**
- **Location:** Bugis
- **Room Types:** Superior, Deluxe, and Loft rooms
- **Amenities:** Free Wi-Fi, fitness center
- **On-Site Facilities:** 24-hour front desk, laundry services
- **Dining Options:** Ginett Restaurant & Wine Bar
- **Service Quality:** Friendly staff with a focus on personal touch
- **Unique Features:** Stylish design with a mix of modern and vintage elements
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 120 per night

**Pros:** Central location, great for young travelers and backpackers  
**Cons:** Rooms are relatively small

### 2. **YOTEL Singapore Orchard Road**
- **Location:** Orchard Road
- **Room Types:** Standard, Premium, and First Class cabins
- **Amenities:** Smart technology in rooms, rooftop pool
- **On-Site Facilities:** 24-hour gym, self-service laundry
- **Dining Options:** Komyuniti restaurant and bar
- **Service Quality:** Efficient and tech-oriented
- **Unique Features:** Automated check-in and futuristic design
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 150 per night

**Pros:** Innovative technology, prime shopping location  
**Cons:** Limited dining options on-site

## Mid-Range Hotels

### 3. **Novotel Singapore Clarke Quay**
- **Location:** Clarke Quay
- **Room Types:** Standard, Superior, and Executive rooms
- **Amenities:** Outdoor pool, fitness center, free Wi-Fi
- **On-Site Facilities:** Business center, kid's club
- **Dining Options:** The SQUARE Restaurant, Lobby Lounge
- **Service Quality:** Attentive staff, family-friendly
- **Unique Features:** Scenic views of the Singapore River
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 200 per night

**Pros:** Great location for nightlife, family-friendly amenities  
**Cons:** Can be noisy due to nearby bars

### 4. **Park Hotel Clarke Quay**
- **Location:** Clarke Quay
- **Room Types:** Deluxe rooms, Premier rooms, and Family rooms
- **Amenities:** Swimming pool, gym, free Wi-Fi
- **On-Site Facilities:** Conference rooms, business lounge
- **Dining Options:** The Coffee Belt, and outdoor bar
- **Service Quality:** Professional and quick service
- **Unique Features:** Close proximity to cultural attractions 
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 220 per night

**Pros:** Riverside location, great for sightseeing  
**Cons:** Limited dining options nearby

## Luxury Hotels

### 5. **Shangri-La Hotel, Singapore**
- **Location:** Orchard Road
- **Room Types:** Tower Wing, Garden Wing, and Valley Wing suites
- **Amenities:** Outdoor pool, spa, tennis courts
- **On-Site Facilities:** Business center, extensive gardens
- **Dining Options:** The Line, Origin Grill, and more
- **Service Quality:** Exceptional, with personalized service
- **Unique Features:** Lush tropical gardens
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 400 per night

**Pros:** Luxurious experience, family-friendly  
**Cons:** High price point

### 6. **The Fullerton Hotel Singapore**
- **Location:** Marina Bay
- **Room Types:** Heritage Room, Courtyard Room, and Executive Suite
- **Amenities:** Infinity pool, spa, fitness center
- **On-Site Facilities:** Business center, event spaces
- **Dining Options:** The Lighthouse Restaurant & Rooftop Bar
- **Service Quality:** Impeccable, attentive staff
- **Unique Features:** Historic building with a rich history
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 450 per night

**Pros:** Stunning architecture, excellent dining  
**Cons:** Pricey for budget travelers

### 7. **Marina Bay Sands**
- **Location:** Marina Bay
- **Room Types:** Deluxe, Premier, and Sands Suites
- **Amenities:** Rooftop infinity pool, spa, shopping mall
- **On-Site Facilities:** Casino, SkyPark, convention center
- **Dining Options:** Celebrity chef restaurants like CUT and db Bistro & Oyster Bar
- **Service Quality:** High-end service, extensive concierge
- **Unique Features:** Iconic rooftop pool with panoramic views
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 600 per night

**Pros:** Iconic skyline views, luxury shopping  
**Cons:** Crowded, especially at the SkyPark

## Ultra-Luxury Hotels

### 8. **Raffles Singapore**
- **Location:** City Hall
- **Room Types:** Palm Court, Grand Hotel Suite, and Presidential Suite
- **Amenities:** Private butler service, spa, swimming pool
- **On-Site Facilities:** Historic Long Bar, luxury boutiques
- **Dining Options:** Raffles Grill, Tiffin Room
- **Service Quality:** World-renowned service quality
- **Unique Features:** Colonial architecture, rich history
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 1,200 per night

**Pros:** Historic charm, ultimate luxury experience  
**Cons:** Very high price point

### 9. **The St. Regis Singapore**
- **Location:** Orchard Road
- **Room Types:** Deluxe, Executive, and St. Regis Suites
- **Amenities:** Butler service, spa, art gallery
- **On-Site Facilities:** Meeting and event spaces
- **Dining Options:** Astor Bar, Brasserie Les Saveurs
- **Service Quality:** Flawless and anticipatory
- **Unique Features:** The famous St. Regis Butler service
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 450 per night

**Pros:** Opulent design, exceptional service  
**Cons:** Can be overwhelming for some travelers

### 10. **Capella Singapore**
- **Location:** Sentosa Island
- **Room Types:** Garden, Ocean View, and Constellation Rooms
- **Amenities:** Spa, three swimming pools, fitness center
- **On-Site Facilities:** Event spaces, horse riding
- **Dining Options:** Cassiopeia and Bob's Bar
- **Service Quality:** Personalized and attentive
- **Unique Features:** Nestled in a lush tropical setting
- **Price:** Starting at SGD 700 per night

**Pros:** Serene environment, family-friendly  
**Cons:** Distance from the city center
