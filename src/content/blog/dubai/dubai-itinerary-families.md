# Family-Friendly Dubai Itinerary: A Week of Adventure for All Ages

Dubai, with its blend of modern marvels and traditional culture, is an exciting destination for families. This itinerary is designed to cater to children of all ages, ensuring that everyone, from toddlers to teens, has a memorable experience. Expect a mix of thrilling adventures, relaxing beach days, and educational activities, all while incorporating essential breaks and dining options.

## Day 1: Arrival and Beach Time

### Morning
- **Arrive in Dubai**: Check into your family-friendly hotel. Recommended options include the **Rove Downtown** or **Atlantis The Palm**, both of which have family amenities.
- **Rest**: Allow for a short rest or nap, especially if you arrive early in the day.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Head to **The Beach at JBR** and grab a bite at **Shake Shack** (kids love their burgers and shakes). Restrooms are located conveniently nearby.
- **Beach Time**: Enjoy the soft sands and shallow waters, perfect for younger children. There are shaded areas and lifeguards on duty.

### Evening
- **Dinner**: Try **TGI Fridays** at JBR, known for its extensive kids' menu.
- **Activities**: After dinner, stroll along the JBR Walk. There are often street performers and open-air entertainment, keeping kids engaged.
  
### Tips for Day 1
- **Transportation**: Use taxis or rideshare apps for easy transportation with children.
- **Rest Stops**: Restrooms are readily available at malls and beaches.

## Day 2: Adventure at Dubai Parks and Resorts

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: Enjoy a family breakfast at your hotel.
- **Activity**: Visit **Legoland Dubai** (tickets start around AED 295 for adults and AED 245 for children). It's perfect for children ages 2-12, with rides and attractions for all energy levels.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Have lunch at **The Market Restaurant** within Legoland, offering kid-friendly meals.
- **Rest**: Kids can enjoy a nap in the stroller or at shaded picnic areas around the park.

### Evening
- **Activity**: Head to **Motiongate Dubai** (tickets included with Legoland ticket). This Hollywood-themed park offers rides for older kids and teens.
- **Dinner**: Dine at **Mamma Mia**, an Italian restaurant inside Motiongate.

### Tips for Day 2
- **Crowd Management**: Arrive early to avoid long lines. Use the app for wait times and show schedules.
- **Accessibility**: Both parks are stroller-friendly with ample rest areas.

## Day 3: Cultural Exploration

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: Start with a hearty breakfast at **Arabian Tea House** in Al Fahidi District.
- **Activity**: Visit the **Dubai Museum** (entry AED 3). This interactive museum is engaging for kids, with exhibits that showcase Dubai’s history.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Enjoy a meal at **Al Fanar Restaurant** for traditional Emirati cuisine.
- **Activity**: Explore the **Dubai Creek** area. Take an abra ride (AED 1), which is fun for all ages.

### Evening
- **Activity**: Visit **Global Village** (seasonal, check dates). It’s a cultural festival with pavilions from around the world, rides, and shows.
- **Dinner**: Sample street food from various pavilions at Global Village.

### Tips for Day 3
- **Safety**: Keep a close eye on children in crowded areas.
- **Rest Areas**: Look for shaded spots at the museum and Global Village for breaks.

## Day 4: Nature and Wildlife Day

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: Have a light breakfast at your hotel.
- **Activity**: Visit **Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo** at Dubai Mall (tickets start at AED 100). It’s educational and visually captivating for kids of all ages.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Dine at **The Cheesecake Factory** in Dubai Mall, which has a large menu with options for all ages.
- **Activity**: Spend the afternoon at **Dubai Dolphinarium** (tickets around AED 120). The shows are entertaining for younger kids.

### Evening
- **Activity**: Head to **Zabeel Park** for an evening stroll. The park has playgrounds and shaded areas.
- **Dinner**: Picnic style with takeout from **Zaroob**, which serves Middle Eastern fast food.

### Tips for Day 4
- **Interactive Learning**: Encourage kids to ask questions during the aquarium visit.
- **Transportation**: Use the Dubai Metro to reach the mall and park easily.

## Day 5: Thrills at Theme Parks

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: A quick breakfast at **Paul Café** in Dubai Mall.
- **Activity**: Visit **IMG Worlds of Adventure** (tickets around AED 300). It's an indoor theme park with zones for various age groups.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Eat at **The Avenger’s Cafe** inside the park.
- **Rest**: Take breaks in shaded areas or at the lounge spots within the park.

### Evening
- **Activity**: Head to **Dubai Parks and Resorts** for a night of entertainment and rides.
- **Dinner**: Enjoy a meal at **Rangoli** for a taste of Indian cuisine.

### Tips for Day 5
- **Hydration**: Keep water bottles handy, especially during theme park visits.
- **Resting Spots**: Use family lounges for comfortable breaks.

## Day 6: Shopping and Relaxation

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: Indulge in breakfast at **Café Bateel**.
- **Activity**: Head to **Mall of the Emirates** for shopping and indoor skiing at **Ski Dubai**. Entry tickets start at AED 250.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Enjoy family-friendly dining at **The Butcher Shop & Grill**.
- **Rest**: Allow kids to nap or relax in designated areas around the mall.

### Evening
- **Activity**: Visit **Kidzania** (tickets around AED 180), where children can role-play different professions.
- **Dinner**: Wrap up the day at **Al Safadi**, offering a wide variety of dishes.

### Tips for Day 6
- **Kids’ Interests**: Let older children choose activities in Kidzania to keep them engaged.
- **Shopping**: Utilize family restrooms and nursing rooms in malls.

## Day 7: Departure Day

### Morning
- **Breakfast**: Enjoy a farewell breakfast at your hotel.
- **Activity**: If time allows, visit **Dubai Fountain** for a morning show. It’s a great way to end your trip.

### Afternoon
- **Lunch**: Quick bites at **The Dubai Mall food court** before heading to the airport.
- **Rest**: Ensure to account for travel time and airport check-in.

### Tips for Departure
- **Packing Snacks**: Pack snacks for kids to keep them happy during travel.
- **Transportation**: Pre-book an airport transfer to avoid last-minute stress.
