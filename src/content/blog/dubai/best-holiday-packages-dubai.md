# The Ultimate Guide to Holiday Packages in Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai, with its stunning skyline, luxurious shopping experiences, and cultural richness, is a top destination for travelers worldwide. With a myriad of holiday packages available, navigating the best option can be daunting. This guide will help you understand the types of packages available, major providers, what's typically included, and how to customize your perfect Dubai getaway.

## Types of Holiday Packages

### All-Inclusive Packages

All-inclusive packages are ideal for travelers seeking convenience. These packages typically cover:

- **Flights**: Roundtrip airfare to Dubai
- **Accommodation**: Hotel stays ranging from budget to luxury
- **Meals**: All meals at hotel restaurants or specified dining venues
- **Tours and Activities**: Guided excursions to major attractions like the Burj Khalifa, Desert Safari, and Dubai Creek
- **Transfers**: Airport pick-up and drop-off

**Typical Cost**: AED 5,000 - 12,000 (USD 1,360 - 3,270) per person

### Flight + Hotel Packages

These are flexible packages that include airfare and accommodation, allowing travelers to choose their activities on the go.

- **Flights**: Roundtrip tickets
- **Accommodation**: A range from budget hostels to five-star resorts
- **Transportation**: Optional car rentals or public transport passes

**Typical Cost**: AED 3,000 - 8,000 (USD 820 - 2,180) per person

### Guided Tours

Perfect for first-time visitors, these packages provide structured itineraries with professional guides.

- **Guides**: Expert guides fluent in multiple languages
- **Itineraries**: Pre-planned visits to landmarks
- **Cultural Experiences**: Traditional performances, local dining

**Typical Cost**: AED 4,000 - 10,000 (USD 1,090 - 2,720) per person

### Self-Drive Packages

For those who enjoy exploring independently, self-drive packages provide rental cars with suggested itineraries.

- **Car Rental**: Includes GPS and unlimited mileage
- **Accommodation**: Flexible booking options
- **Suggested Itineraries**: Customized maps highlighting top spots

**Typical Cost**: AED 3,500 - 9,000 (USD 950 - 2,450) per person

**Tip**: Ensure your international driving permit is valid in the UAE before opting for self-drive packages.

## Top Package Providers

1. **Emirates Holidays**: Known for luxury and premium experiences.
2. **Travel Republic**: Offers budget-friendly options with customizable packages.
3. **Viator**: Specializes in guided tours with local experts.
4. **Expedia**: Provides a wide range of flight + hotel deals.
5. **Arabian Adventures**: Renowned for desert safaris and cultural tours.

**Insider Tip**: Subscribe to newsletters from these providers for exclusive discounts and early-bird offers.

## What's Typically Included in Packages

- **Accommodation**: Varies from 3-star hotels to luxury resorts
- **Transfers**: Convenient airport transfers
- **Meals**: Depending on package type, breakfast-only or all meals
- **Activities**: Some packages include entry to major attractions
- **Travel Insurance**: Often included or offered at a discounted rate

## Price Ranges and How to Compare Packages

When comparing packages, consider the following:

- **Inclusions**: Make sure all desired activities are covered.
- **Accommodation Quality**: Check hotel reviews and ratings.
- **Flexibility**: Look for packages that allow modifications.
- **Customer Support**: Ensure easy access to assistance during your trip.

**Typical Price Range**: AED 3,000 - 12,000 (USD 820 - 3,270) per person, depending on package inclusions and duration.

**Hidden Costs to Watch For**:

- **Resort Fees**: Often not included in the package price
- **Tourist Taxes**: Charged per night at hotels
- **Optional Excursions**: Not included in base package price

**Insider Tip**: Always read the fine print of the package for any additional fees or surcharges.

## Best Time to Book

The ideal time to book a Dubai package is during the cooler months from **November to March**. Booking 3-6 months in advance can secure the best rates and availability.

**Tip**: Consider traveling during Ramadan for fewer crowds and unique cultural experiences.

## Package vs DIY Cost Comparison

**Package Benefits**:

- **Convenience**: Less planning required
- **Cost Savings**: Bundled rates often cheaper than individual bookings
- **Assistance**: Support from travel agents and guides

**DIY Travel**:

- **Flexibility**: Total control over every aspect of the trip
- **Customization**: Tailor each activity and accommodation to your preference
- **Local Experiences**: More opportunity to explore lesser-known spots

**Cost Consideration**: DIY can sometimes be costlier due to fluctuating prices on flights and accommodations, especially during peak seasons.

## Tips for Customizing Packages

- **Prioritize Interests**: Choose packages that align with your top interests, whether it's shopping, adventure, or culture.
- **Mix and Match**: Opt for a basic package and add-on specific tours or experiences.
- **Upgrade Options**: Consider upgrading flights or accommodations for a more luxurious experience.

**Insider Tip**: Work with a travel agent who knows Dubai well to create a bespoke itinerary that fits your needs and budget.
