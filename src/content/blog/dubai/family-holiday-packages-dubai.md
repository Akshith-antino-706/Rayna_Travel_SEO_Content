# Family Holiday Package Guide for Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is a dazzling destination that offers a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation for families. With its stunning skyscrapers, sun-kissed beaches, and an array of family-friendly activities, Dubai is an ideal location for an unforgettable family vacation. This guide will help you navigate the myriad of family holiday packages available in Dubai, providing valuable insights and tips for an enjoyable experience.

## What to Look For in Family Packages

When choosing a family holiday package in Dubai, it's essential to consider several factors to ensure the trip is enjoyable for both parents and children. Here are some key aspects to look for:

- **Kids Eat Free**: Many hotels offer packages where children eat free at on-site restaurants. This can significantly reduce your dining expenses.
- **Family Rooms**: Look for accommodations with family rooms or suites that offer separate sleeping areas for parents and children.
- **Kids' Clubs**: Resorts with dedicated kids' clubs provide supervised activities, allowing parents some leisure time.
- **Age-Appropriate Inclusions**: Ensure the package includes activities suitable for the age range of your children.
- **Convenient Location**: Choose a hotel that is centrally located to minimize travel time to popular attractions.

## Top Family Package Providers

Several travel agencies and online platforms offer comprehensive family packages to Dubai. Here are a few top providers:

- **Emirates Holidays**: Known for its extensive range of family-friendly packages, including flights and accommodation.
- **TUI**: Offers tailored family packages with a focus on all-inclusive resorts.
- **Expedia**: Provides customizable packages, allowing families to choose their preferred flights, hotels, and activities.
- **Dubai Parks and Resorts Packages**: Perfect for families looking to explore Dubai's theme parks with bundled hotel stays.

## Cost Considerations

### School Holiday vs. Off-Peak Pricing

The cost of a family holiday in Dubai can vary significantly depending on the time of year:

- **School Holidays**: Prices are typically higher during school holidays, with an average package cost for a family of four ranging from AED 15,000 to AED 20,000 (approx. USD 4,080 to USD 5,440) for a week-long stay.
- **Off-Peak Season**: Traveling during the off-peak months, such as May to September, can reduce costs by up to 30%, with packages starting at AED 10,000 (approx. USD 2,720).

**Insider Tip:** Consider traveling during the shoulder months (April and October) for a balance of good weather and reasonable prices.

## Tips for Booking with Children

- **Plan Ahead**: Book flights, accommodations, and activities well in advance to secure the best deals, especially during peak seasons.
- **Check Inclusions**: Ensure the package includes key amenities like airport transfers, meals, and tickets to major attractions.
- **Be Flexible**: Consider flexible booking options or travel insurance to manage unexpected changes.
- **Pack Smart**: Bring essential items such as sunscreen, hats, and swimwear, considering the sunny climate.
- **Engage the Kids**: Involve your children in planning to get them excited about the trip and ensure activities cater to their interests.

**Tip:** Always keep a digital copy of your travel documents and itinerary for easy access.

A family holiday in Dubai offers an exciting mix of activities and experiences that cater to all ages.
