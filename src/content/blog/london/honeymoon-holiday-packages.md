# Romantic Honeymoon Packages in London: The Ultimate Guide

London, with its enchanting blend of history and modernity, is a dreamy destination for couples embarking on their honeymoon. Offering an array of luxurious accommodations, romantic activities, and exquisite dining options, the city provides an unforgettable backdrop for lovebirds. In this guide, we will explore some of the best honeymoon packages available, including luxury stays, romantic experiences, and practical tips to make your honeymoon extraordinary.

## Luxury Accommodations

### 1. The Shard - Shangri-La Hotel

**Duration:** 3 Nights  
**Pricing:** Starting from £650 per night  
**Operator:** Shangri-La Hotels

The Shangri-La Hotel at The Shard offers breathtaking views of the London skyline from its luxurious rooms. Each room is designed with elegance and comfort, providing a perfect romantic getaway. Honeymoon packages typically include:

- Welcome champagne
- Room decoration with rose petals
- Daily breakfast for two
- Access to the hotel’s infinity pool and spa

### 2. The Dorchester

**Duration:** 2 Nights  
**Pricing:** Starting from £1,200 per night  
**Operator:** The Dorchester Collection

The Dorchester is synonymous with luxury. Nestled in Mayfair, this iconic hotel provides an opulent atmosphere. Packages can include:

- Private dinner in your room or at the hotel’s Michelin-starred restaurant
- Couples’ spa treatment (50% discount on a second treatment)
- A personalized anniversary cake

### 3. The Ritz London

**Duration:** 3 Nights  
**Pricing:** Starting from £1,000 per night  
**Operator:** The Ritz London

Famed for its grandeur, The Ritz offers honeymooners a touch of royalty. Their honeymoon package might include:

- Suite upgrade (subject to availability)
- Afternoon tea for two
- A private tour of the hotel’s historic art collection

## Romantic Activities

### 1. Thames River Cruise

A romantic dinner cruise on the Thames River is a must. Companies like Bateaux London offer packages starting at £70 per person, including a three-course meal and live entertainment while you glide past iconic landmarks like the Tower Bridge and the Houses of Parliament.

### 2. Private London Eye Capsule

For a unique experience, book a private capsule on the London Eye. Packages start at £450 and include champagne and a personalized photo package. It’s a romantic way to see the city from a new perspective.

### 3. Stroll Through Kew Gardens

Explore the beautiful Kew Gardens, a UNESCO World Heritage site, with an entrance fee of just £19.50 per person. The stunning landscapes provide a perfect backdrop for a leisurely walk or a romantic picnic.

## Special Meals

### 1. Dinner at Clos Maggiore

Often dubbed the most romantic restaurant in London, Clos Maggiore offers a cozy atmosphere and exquisite French cuisine. Expect to pay around £70-£100 per person for a three-course meal complete with wine pairings.

### 2. Sky Garden

Book a table at the Sky Garden for breathtaking views and a unique dining experience. Dinner prices range from £50-£70 per person. The ambiance is perfect for a romantic evening.

## Spa Treatments

### 1. The Spa at Mandarin Oriental

Indulge in a couple’s massage at The Spa, where packages start at £350. Treatments include full access to their facilities, including a vitality pool, steam room, and relaxation areas.

### 2. The Bliss Spa at W London

This trendy spa offers a range of couples treatments, with packages starting at £250, including massages and skincare treatments.

## Couple Experiences

### 1. Private Photo Shoot

Capture your honeymoon memories with a private photoshoot around London. Companies like Flytographer offer packages starting at £250 for a one-hour session at iconic spots like Tower Bridge or the South Bank.

### 2. Cooking Class

Take a cooking class together at the Cookery School in Little Portland Street. Classes range from £120-£200 per couple, allowing you to learn to prepare traditional British dishes while bonding over a shared love for food.

## All-Inclusive vs. Partial Packages

### All-Inclusive Packages

All-inclusive packages typically cover your accommodation, meals, activities, and some extras. This is perfect for those who want a stress-free experience without worrying about additional costs. For example, The Dorchester offers an all-inclusive honeymoon package that includes several romantic dinners, spa treatments, and activities.

### Partial Packages

Partial packages allow for more customization, letting you choose the specific experiences you want. This is ideal if you have particular interests or wish to explore the city more independently. Operators like Secret Escapes frequently offer deals where you can mix and match your accommodation with activities.

## Tips for Selecting Your Honeymoon Package

1. **Set a Budget:** Determine your budget upfront and look for packages that fit within that range. Consider not just accommodation but also meals and activities.
  
2. **Research Operators:** Look for reputable operators with good reviews. Websites like TripAdvisor, Expedia, and Booking.com are great resources.

3. **Customization Options:** Look for packages that allow for personalization, such as room decorations, special meals, or specific activities.

4. **Book in Advance:** Popular honeymoon destinations can fill up quickly. Book your package at least three to six months in advance for the best options.

5. **Ask About Honeymoon Perks:** Always inquire about special honeymoon services like complimentary upgrades, room decorations, and anniversary cakes.

## Making Your Honeymoon Special

- **Plan Surprises:** Coordinate with your hotel for surprise arrangements, like a romantic candlelit dinner or a surprise picnic at a park.
  
- **Stay Connected:** Use apps like Google Maps to plan your days effectively and discover hidden gems around the city.

- **Document Your Journey:** Create a travel journal or blog to document your experiences together. It’s a wonderful way to reflect on your honeymoon in the future.

### Conclusion

London offers a myriad of romantic honeymoon packages that cater to various budgets and preferences. From luxurious accommodations to enchanting activities, the city is a perfect canvas for your love story. By selecting the right package, considering your interests, and planning some surprises, you can create a honeymoon that you and your partner will cherish forever. Embrace the romance of London and make memories that will last a lifetime!
