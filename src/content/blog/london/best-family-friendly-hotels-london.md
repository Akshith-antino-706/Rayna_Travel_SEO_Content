# London’s Best Family-Friendly Hotels: A Guide to Kid-Friendly Accommodations

London is a vibrant city teeming with history, culture, and countless activities perfect for families. When traveling with children, choosing the right hotel can make all the difference. Below, we’ve curated a list of the best family-friendly hotels in London, detailing their offerings, accommodations, and insider tips for a smooth stay.

## 1. The Londoner

### Family Room Configurations
The Londoner offers spacious family rooms that can accommodate up to five guests. These rooms come with two queen beds and a sofa bed, ensuring ample space for the entire family.

### Kid Clubs and Activities
The hotel features an engaging kids' club that offers activities tailored for various age groups, including arts and crafts, movie nights, and scavenger hunts.

### Playground Facilities
While there are no on-site playgrounds, the hotel is located near St. James's Park, perfect for outdoor play.

### Pool Safety Features
The hotel boasts a stunning rooftop pool with shallow areas for younger children, complete with safety equipment and lifeguards on duty.

### Restaurant Options for Kids
The on-site restaurant, the Whitcomb's, offers a children's menu with healthy options, including mini burgers, pasta, and fruit platters.

### Babysitting Services
Parents can request professional babysitting services, allowing them to enjoy a night out in the city.

### Additional Family Amenities
- **Crib Availability:** Cribs can be requested at no extra charge.
- **High Chairs:** High chairs are available in the restaurant.
- **Connecting Rooms:** Connecting rooms are available upon request for larger families.

### Insider Tips
When booking, inquire about special family packages that may include breakfast and activity vouchers.

## 2. The Athenaeum Hotel & Residences

### Family Room Configurations
The Athenaeum provides family suites with separate living areas and kitchenettes, making it ideal for families seeking more space and convenience.

### Kid Clubs and Activities
The hotel has a dedicated children's program, offering activities that include arts and crafts, movie nights, and nature walks in nearby parks.

### Playground Facilities
Located just a short walk from Green Park, the hotel provides easy access to playgrounds and open spaces.

### Pool Safety Features
The indoor pool features a shallow end suitable for kids, and floatation devices are available upon request.

### Restaurant Options for Kids
The hotel’s restaurant offers a kid-friendly menu with options such as fish fingers, chicken nuggets, and desserts like ice cream sundaes.

### Babysitting Services
The Athenaeum also provides babysitting services, allowing parents to enjoy London’s nightlife.

### Additional Family Amenities
- **Crib Availability:** Cribs are available and can be set up in rooms upon arrival.
- **High Chairs:** High chairs are readily available in the restaurant.
- **Connecting Rooms:** Connecting rooms can be arranged, perfect for larger families.

### Insider Tips
Ask about the ‘Family Fun’ package, which often includes tickets for local attractions.

## 3. Radisson Blu Edwardian, Kenilworth

### Family Room Configurations
This hotel offers family rooms with one king bed and a sofa bed, accommodating up to four guests comfortably.

### Kid Clubs and Activities
While there is no formal kids’ club, the hotel provides an activity kit on check-in for younger guests, including coloring books and toys.

### Playground Facilities
The hotel is close to Russell Square Gardens, which features a playground for children.

### Pool Safety Features
The hotel has a small indoor pool that is monitored by staff, ensuring safety for children while swimming.

### Restaurant Options for Kids
The restaurant offers a dedicated children’s menu featuring pizza, pasta, and a selection of healthy options.

### Babysitting Services
Babysitting services are available for a fee, allowing parents to explore the city worry-free.

### Additional Family Amenities
- **Crib Availability:** Cribs can be provided for infants at no additional charge.
- **High Chairs:** Available upon request in the dining area.
- **Connecting Rooms:** Families can book connecting rooms for larger needs.

### Insider Tips
Check for seasonal family packages that might include discounts on attractions and meals.

## 4. Hilton London Bankside

### Family Room Configurations
The Hilton London Bankside offers spacious suites with separate living areas, ideal for families needing extra space.

### Kid Clubs and Activities
The hotel features a kids’ club during school holidays, where children can engage in fun activities while parents relax.

### Playground Facilities
There are nearby parks with playgrounds, including the lovely Borough Market area, perfect for a family outing.

### Pool Safety Features
The hotel has a family-friendly indoor pool with a shallow end and lifeguards present.

### Restaurant Options for Kids
The on-site restaurant serves a children’s menu with options like chicken skewers and fish and chips, ensuring picky eaters are well catered to.

### Babysitting Services
Babysitting services are available upon request for an additional fee.

### Additional Family Amenities
- **Crib Availability:** Cribs are available at no extra charge.
- **High Chairs:** High chairs can be provided in the restaurant.
- **Connecting Rooms:** Connecting rooms are available for larger families.

### Insider Tips
Ask about early check-in and late check-out options to make traveling with kids easier.

## Tips for Choosing Family Hotels

### What to Consider
1. **Room Configurations:** Ensure the room can accommodate your family size comfortably.
2. **Kid-Friendly Amenities:** Look for on-site restaurants with kids’ menus, pools with safety features, and available babysitting services.
3. **Location:** Choose a hotel close to parks or attractions to minimize travel time.
4. **Age-Appropriate Activities:** Make sure the hotel provides activities suitable for your children's age group.

### Questions to Ask When Booking
- Are cribs and high chairs available?
- Is there a children’s program or activities for kids?
- What safety measures are in place for pools?
- Are there connecting rooms available?

### Dealing with Kids' Needs
- Pack snacks and entertainment options for downtime in the hotel.
- Maintain a routine, especially during mealtimes and bedtimes.
- Be prepared to adapt plans based on your kids’ energy levels.

### Final Thoughts
Traveling with kids in London can be a delightful experience when you choose the right family-friendly hotel. From spacious accommodations to exciting activities, the hotels listed above provide everything you need for a memorable family vacation in this iconic city. Remember to plan ahead, ask the right questions, and most importantly, enjoy your time together!
