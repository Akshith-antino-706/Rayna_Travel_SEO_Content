# Essential Emergency Contact Numbers for Tokyo

When traveling to Tokyo, it’s crucial to be prepared for any emergencies that may arise. This guide provides a comprehensive list of emergency contact numbers, embassy information, hospitals, and other useful resources to ensure a safe and enjoyable trip. 

## Emergency Services

### Police
- **Emergency Number**: 110
- **English Speakers**: Limited. It’s advisable to have your location and emergency details ready in Japanese.
- **What to Say**: "Help! I need the police." (助けて！警察が必要です。)

### Ambulance
- **Emergency Number**: 119
- **English Speakers**: Limited, but operators may understand basic English.
- **What to Say**: “I need an ambulance.” (救急車が必要です。)

### Fire Department
- **Emergency Number**: 119
- **Note**: This number also serves for fire emergencies.
- **What to Say**: “There is a fire!” (火事です！)

### Poison Control
- **Phone Number**: 03-5285-9090
- **English Speakers**: Limited. Use simple phrases if necessary.
- **What to Say**: "I need help with a poisoning." (中毒に助けが必要です。)

### Tourist Police
- **Phone Number**: 03-5774-4874
- **Notes**: The Tokyo Tourist Police are available to assist visitors with various issues.
- **English Speakers**: Yes.
- **What to Say**: "I need help as a tourist." (観光客として助けが必要です。)

### Tokyo English Lifeline (TELL)
- **Phone Number**: 03-5774-0992
- **Services**: Provides free, anonymous, and confidential support.
- **English Speakers**: Yes.

## How to Call in Tokyo

When calling within Japan, the area code for Tokyo is **03**. For international dialing to Tokyo from abroad, use the following format:

- **Dial**: +81 3 + [local number]
  - **Example**: To call Tokyo Medical University Hospital, dial +81 3 3342 6111.

### Language Considerations
While many Japanese people, especially in urban areas like Tokyo, may understand basic English, it's always helpful to have key phrases written down or use a translation app. 

## Non-Emergency Useful Numbers

### Taxi Services
- **Tokyo Taxi**: 03-5766-9111
- **English Speakers**: Limited; consider using apps like Uber or JapanTaxi for more convenience.

### Roadside Assistance
- **JAF (Japan Automobile Federation)**: 0570-00-8139
- **Note**: They provide English-speaking assistance.

### Tourist Information
- **Tokyo Tourist Information Center**: 03-5320-7890
- **Location**: Various centers, with the main one in Shinjuku.
- **Services**: Offers maps, brochures, and assistance in English.

## Conclusion

Having access to emergency services and contact numbers while traveling is essential for a safe trip. Tokyo is well-equipped with resources, and knowing how to reach them can make a significant difference. Familiarize yourself with this information and enjoy your time in this vibrant city!
