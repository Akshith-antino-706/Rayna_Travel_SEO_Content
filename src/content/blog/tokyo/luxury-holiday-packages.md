# Ultimate Luxury Holiday Packages in Tokyo: An Exclusive Guide

Tokyo, a vibrant blend of ancient tradition and cutting-edge modernity, offers a luxury holiday experience like no other. From 5-star accommodations to bespoke experiences, this city is a treasure trove of exclusive opportunities for the discerning traveler. Below, we will explore some of the most luxurious holiday packages available in Tokyo, including details on accommodations, unique experiences, and practical tips for making your trip unforgettable.

## Luxurious Accommodations

### 1. The Peninsula Tokyo

**Overview**: Located in the Marunouchi district, The Peninsula Tokyo is renowned for its blend of modern luxury and traditional Japanese hospitality.

**Price**: Starting at approximately $700 per night.

**Inclusions**:
- Luxurious rooms with sweeping views of the Imperial Palace.
- Access to the spa, fitness center, and swimming pool.
- Complimentary breakfast at The Lobby or the restaurant.

### 2. Aman Tokyo

**Overview**: Aman Tokyo is a sanctuary in the heart of the city, offering unparalleled service and a serene atmosphere.

**Price**: Starting at approximately $1,500 per night.

**Inclusions**:
- Spacious rooms with contemporary Japanese design.
- Access to the Aman Spa, featuring exclusive treatments.
- Daily breakfast and personalized wellness consultations.

### 3. Mandarin Oriental Tokyo

**Overview**: This stunning hotel offers panoramic views of the Tokyo skyline and impeccable service.

**Price**: Starting at approximately $800 per night.

**Inclusions**:
- Luxurious accommodations with modern amenities.
- Access to the spa and wellness facilities.
- Fine dining at the Michelin-starred restaurant.

## Exclusive Experiences

### Private Guided Tours

**Overview**: An exclusive private guide can elevate your Tokyo experience, providing insider knowledge and personalized itineraries.

**Operators**: A representative of Black Tomato or Scott Dunn can tailor your experience.

**Price**: $500-$1,000 per day, depending on the guide's expertise and duration.

### Helicopter Tours

**Overview**: Experience Tokyo from a bird's-eye view with a private helicopter tour.

**Operators**: Tokyo Helicopter Tours offers packages that include scenic routes over landmarks like Tokyo Tower and the Rainbow Bridge.

**Price**: Starting at $2,000 per flight for up to 4 passengers.

### Luxury Dining

Tokyo is home to some of the world’s finest dining experiences, including:

- **Sukiyabashi Jiro**: A Michelin-starred sushi destination, where a meal can cost around $300 per person.
- **Narisawa**: Known for its innovative approach to Japanese cuisine, with tasting menus priced around $400.

## Bespoke Services and Concierge

### Personalized Itineraries

Luxury travel operators like **Abercrombie & Kent** offer personalized itineraries tailored to your specific interests, whether it’s art, food, or history.

### Concierge Services

Concierge services can assist with:

- Restaurant reservations at exclusive venues.
- Arranging private transportation, including luxury cars and chauffeurs.
- Securing tickets to sold-out shows or events.

### Special Requests

Operators often accommodate special requests, such as arranging a private tea ceremony, exclusive access to art galleries, or even shopping experiences with a personal stylist.

## Unique Ultra-Luxury Experiences in Tokyo

1. **Private Sumo Wrestling Experience**: Attend a private sumo wrestling match where you can meet and interact with wrestlers. Prices start at $1,500 per person.

2. **Exclusive Geisha Dinner**: Enjoy an intimate dinner with a Geisha, learning about this unique cultural art. Packages start around $600 per person.

3. **Luxury Shopping Tour**: Spend a day with a personal shopper at luxury boutiques in Ginza, with prices starting at $1,000.

## Pricing and Payment Details

### Package Pricing

Luxury packages generally range from $5,000 to $15,000 per person, depending on the duration, inclusions, and level of exclusivity. 

### Deposit Requirements

Most luxury operators require a deposit of 30% to secure your booking, with the balance due 30 days prior to travel.

### Cancellation Policies

Cancellation policies vary but typically allow for full refunds if canceled 30 days in advance. Cancellations made within 30 days may incur a penalty of up to 100% of the total trip cost.

## What Separates Luxury Packages from Standard Packages

### Personalized Attention

Luxury packages offer a high level of personalized service, ensuring that every aspect of your trip is tailored to your preferences.

### Exclusive Access

With luxury packages, you gain access to exclusive experiences and venues that are not available to standard tourists.

### High-End Amenities

Luxury accommodations and experiences typically come with premium amenities, such as private pools, gourmet meals, and top-tier concierge services.

Tokyo offers an unparalleled luxury holiday experience, with a variety of packages that cater to individual tastes and preferences.
